For Immediate Release

Friday, March 27th, 2009
Made Event and AM Only present
ONE NIGHT ONLY…
an AM Only Event
Karu & Y, Miami

Made Event and AM Only return to Miami’s mega-club Karu & Y this year for the 5th installment of One Night Only for one of the biggest parties of the week with a bigger lineup, brighter stars, and top-notch new talent to be discovered. AM Only founder & president, Paul Morris, reflects upon this year’s mega-event and says, “As AM Only continues to grow I'm excited that we are able to showcase such a wide range of talent at this year's WMC party.  In the past year we have welcomed many new and exciting artists to the roster as well as seen continued growth from our developing acts.  And of course, there will be those that have been with AM Only since day one, which is what I think makes this event really special for me, the company and the fans of electronic music.”

For this One Night Only, AM Only will bring you exclusive DJ pairings – both bigger & bolder than in previous years! This will be the only opportunity to bear witness and hear these special tag-team sets, the likes of which have never been seen before.

The breathtaking and opulent Karu & Y is located in the heart of Miami’s expanding arts and entertainment downtown district. The newly re-opened 42,000 square foot venue provides the ultimate entertainment playground with a few Zen twists. From the entryway’s signature waterfalls, enter into the vast layout of 5 distinct areas, including the Y Ultralounge and Garden, the newly dubbed Champagne Lounge, and Buddha Club with its far east inspired décor, stretching out onto the outdoor Buddha Gardens with its flowing river, white tented cabanas and lush greenery. Immerse yourself in this ultra-trendy, sleek nightclub with today's and tomorrow's biggest dance music stars.
