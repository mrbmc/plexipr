For Immediate Release

Thursday, March 26th, 2009
Above & Beyond present Anjunabeats
a Made Event
@ Karu & Y, Miami

Above & Beyond and the highly respected Anjunabeats label have announced their WMC ’09 plans, with a party at Karu & Y on Thursday March 26th,  a Made Event.  For this exclusive event Anujunabeats will be hosting two rooms at Karu & Y.  Room 1 will feature Anjunabeats label heads Above & Beyond along with Cosmic Gate, Martin Roth,  Signalrunners, Boom Jinx, and very special guest Josh Gabriel.  Room 2 will be hosted by Anjunadeep, the sub label behind the recent Anjunadeep:01 album.  The room will feature Jody Wisternoff of Way Out West along with Anjunadeep rising stars Electrobios, Liluca, and James Grant.

In a short period of time, Above & Beyond’s Anjunadeep imprint has grown from being a promising sibling to the Anjunabeats label, to a dynamic and influential imprint in its own right, pioneering a sound somewhere between house and trance.  About the label’s sound, Above & Beyond’s Tony McGuinness said: "The road between house and trance used to be a dusty, barren path, visited by only the truly brave. But in the last few years it has become an exciting, bustling place with constant traffic going both ways. Anjunadeep is that lovely, shiny building on the left with all the people looking in the window." Winning fans such as James Zabiela, Desyn Masiello, Eric Prydz and Laurent Garnier along the way, Anjunadeep recently received a nomination for "Best Record Label" at DJ Magazine’s Best of British Awards 2008.

The sub label's debut compilation, Anjunadeep:01 features Anjunadeep stalwarts Jaytech, Michael Cassette (aka Komytea), Interplay, Joonas Hahmo and recent high profile addition 16 Bit Lolitas.  They are joined by newer faces like Colombia's Liluca, Russia's Proff and Canadian Paul Keeley, as Above & Beyond conjure up two CDs of spirited melodic house music. A special treat comes in the form of Above & Beyond’s own remix of Radiohead's "Reckoner”, officially licensed via XL Recordings for the physical format only.

The breathtaking and opulent Karu & Y is located in the heart of Miami’s expanding arts and entertainment downtown district. The newly re-opened 42,000 square foot venue, provides the ultimate entertainment playground with a few Zen twists. From the entryway’s signature waterfalls enter into the vast layout, including the Y Ultralounge and Garden, the newly dubbed Champagne Lounge, and Buddha Club with its far east inspired décor. Immerse yourself in this ultra-trendy, sleek nightclub with today's and tomorrow's biggest dance music stars.

‘Anjunadeep:01’ is out now on Ultra Records.
