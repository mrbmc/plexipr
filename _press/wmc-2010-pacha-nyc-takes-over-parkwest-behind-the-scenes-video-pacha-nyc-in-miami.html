FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE


MIAMI 2010
PACHA NYC TAKES OVER PARKWEST
www.PachaNYC.com/Miami2010

Watch behind the scenes video from Pacha NYC in Miami WMC 2009: Part I

New York City mega club Pacha NYC returns to Miami’s Parkwest Nightclub for Winter Music Conference 2010.  Continuing from last year’s successful series, which featured the likes of DJ Boris, Marco Carola, and Victor Calderone, Pacha NYC ups the ante and brings a new batch of bigger and bolder events to the downtown Miami hot spot.

Kicking off on Wednesday night with DJ heavyweight Danny Tenaglia and special guest Paco Osuna, Parkwest will feature some of WMC’s definitive events.  Thursday, March 25th brings the renowned Nervous Records and a slew of their top DJ’s including Austin Leeds, Starkillers, Behrouz, Chus & Ceballos, and Oscar G.  Friday, March 26th see’s and exclusive team up between Pacha NYC and UK's Mixmag, featuring the international superstar front man for the Black Eyed Peas Will.i.am with Boyz Noize, D. Ramirez and many more unannounced superstar DJs. Then on Saturday, New York City house prodigies The Martinez Brothers will get things going while perennial U.S. favorite Jonathan Peters brings it home for a special late night session. To close-out the marathon week, NYC award-winning party Asseteria brings it hedonist ways to Miami for one final blow-out.

The 6,000 square-foot Parkwest features multiple bars and VIP areas throughout the venue.  With striking contemporary photo murals, state-of-the-art lighting systems, multiple video screens including a giant LED wall, and Dynacord Cobra soundsystem, Parkwest has been specifically designed to enhance the multi-sensory experience of its guests.

For more info, please go to the Pacha NYC Miami 2010 mini-site: www.pachanyc.com/miami2010

Wednesday, March 24th, 2010
Pacha NYC presents
BE YOURSELF MIAMI
DANNY TENAGLIA
& PACO OSUNA
$40 advance tickets

One name which remains synonymous with Winter Music Conference is Danny Tenaglia. His parties are a legendary staple of WMC, renowned for his long and definitive sets which hypnotize audiences with deep, dark house from the first beat to the last. Danny returns to Miami this year accompanied by Barcelona techno heavyweight Paco Osuna whose unique style and vibrant stage presence drive international crowds into throbbing frenzies. These two turntable masters will kick the week off right, taking you through a exotic musical landscape and giving a taste of what to expect from Pacha NYC & Parkwest during the week.


Thursday, March 25th, 2010
Nervous Records in association with Pacha NYC present
NERVOUS ALL NITE!
$25 advance tickets

As one of the longest standing independent record labels in the US, Nervous Records has built a reputation through a willingness to take chances on new sounds and talent.  Now, almost 20 years later, the label boasts a roster and catalogue which is unrivaled throughout the industry. Nervous Records brings a collection of their finest representatives for their exclusive label party.  Austin Leeds, Starkillers, Honey Dijon, Behrouz, Pacha NYC residents Chus & Ceballos, King Britt, Theo, Richie Santana, Peter Bailey, Tom Stephan, David Tort, Oscar G, Ralph Falcon, and Baggi Begovic will all represent the house music powerhouse on this night.


Friday, March 26th, 2010
MixMag in association with Pacha NYC present
Mixmag Party
WILL.I.AM (Black Eyed Peas), BOYS NOIZE, D.RAMIREZ
& Many More Unannounced Superstar DJs!
$35 advance tickets

Expect only the cream of the crop when North America's best superclub Pacha NYC and the world's biggest dance music and clubbing magazine Mixmag team up for a night of party mayhem.  The world’s leading authority on club culture and the country’s premier mega club join forces with an event that can only be described as "not to be missed." The night will feature music from international superstar and Black Eyed Peas frontman Will.i.am, electro-tech DJ/producer extraordinaire Boyz Noize, topped off with the always reliable D. Ramirez. Plus super secret unannounced DJs, too big we can't say!

Saturday, March 27th, 2010
Pacha NYC presents
THE MARTINEZ BROTHERS
JONATHAN PETERS (Late Night)
$35 advance tickets
(Ticket Admission includes entry to both TMB & Jonathan Peters late night)

Attendees will witness a musical double-header of epic proportions between the beloved New York club scene veteran Jonathan Peters and house music wonder kids The Martinez Brothers. After a brief hiatus, TMB hit heavy with their follow-up "Don't No Yet" EP on Objektivity to last year's slammer with Argy "Debbie Downer", just in time for WMC. On one of the biggest night's of conference, The Martinez Brothers will get things going while Jonathan takes over for a late night showcase.   Expect two banging sets causing some serious stirs on the dance floor. This one will have you dancing into the wee morning, guaranteed.

Sunday, March 28th, 2010
RobPromotions in association with Pacha NYC presents
Asseteria Invades Miami!
CHRISS VARGAS
& FRIENDS
$20 advance tickets

New York's biggest Sunday party invades Miami when Asseteria touches down at Parkwest. Chriss Vargas and some very special guests will bring the sounds of the city's longest running Sunday night dance party to close out WMC 2010.
