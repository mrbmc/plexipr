FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Thursday, May 14th, 2009
Made Event presents
ROBBIE RIVERA
(Juicy NYC)
With Elevator People

Made Event presents Miami DJ legend and Juicy Music label’s main man, Robbie Rivera at Cielo on Thursday, May 14th, 2009.

With his infectious basslines, body shakin’ sexy vocals, Robbie Rivera brings a brand new Juicy Ibiza compilation on Black Hole Recordings due for release on June 18th. In conjunction with the magical, White Isle of Ibiza, Robbie is bringing his Juicy party with special guests at the world’s biggest nightclub, Privilege – this summer every Tuesday.

In 2008, his second artist album Star Quality, fully produced by Robbie Rivera himself, features ten brand new signature Rivera bangers, including singles “Back to Zero” and “Move, Move”, which have gained world-wide recognition with its crowd-pleasing addictive sing-a-long melodies. His latest new single “In Too Deep” with remixes by EDX, Santiago Cortes and more is out on Juicy Music and his remix of David Guetta’s smash hit “Everytime We Touch” is out now on Ultra Records.

Rivera’s distinctive brand of sexy, pounding, dirty and electro-tinged house continues to ignite dancefloors across the globe and weekly on his Juicy Show Saturday nights from 10pm-Midnight on XM Satellite Radio (BPM Channel 81). He is currently working on a new album for 2009 planned for a September release with a new single is due out this summer.

From gracing dance floors all over the world; his Foam/Juicy party at Amnesia, Ibiza, to his hometown of Miami Juicy Beach shindig at Nikki Beach and Area Sessions @ London’s Area 51 just to name a slew of clubs from across the globe. The legendary, renowned DJ/producer will hit the turntables, this time in the Big Apple for a Juicy night at Cielo.

Robbie Rivera's Star Quality is out now on Beatport and iTunes.
