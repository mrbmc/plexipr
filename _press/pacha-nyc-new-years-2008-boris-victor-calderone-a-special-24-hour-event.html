New Years 2008 will feature New York's only 24 hour party with Pacha's over-the-top debauchery, spectacular sound, quality production, dazzling lighting and two of NYC's biggest DJs!

Boris continues to seduce the masses with his marathon sets at his ongoing Pacha New York. Born in St. Petersburg, Russia, Boris is known for playing 12hour marathon sets of pumping-hard, driving house music that has become synonymous with "the New York sound." With remixes for Janet Jackson, Thalia, Anastacia, Jewel and Pink under his belt, Boris' latest mixed "Believe II" album is out now on Moist Music.

Victor Calderone's prowess as a DJ lies in his ability to blend the old with the new, the crowd favorite with the obscure, and to span genres from banging tribal to techy minimal with grace, the right amount of sexiness and loads of energy. For 20 plus years, Calderone has achieved an amazing career of successful residencies, chart-topping productions and star-studded remixes. With an early cult following and underground vibe, Victor shot to dance music stardom with massive hits like "Give It Up," "Beat Me Harder," and "Resonate." As a producer, his natural studio talent attracted some very big attention, leading to remix work with some of pop music's most popular artists, including Depeche Mode, Goldfrapp, Beyonce, Sting and Madonna.
