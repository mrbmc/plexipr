Nicole Moudaber ‘Somewhere In Our Minds’ EP on Truesoul Records
Upcoming Tour Dates including Bestival Toronto & Spring Awakening Music Festival

Nicole Moudaber at Movement Detroit 2015. Photo credit: Trevor Dernai

Just ahead of  Nicole Moudaber’s set at the inaugural Bestival Toronto next Friday, June 12 she releases a new EP, ‘Somewhere In Our Minds’ on June 8 via Adam Beyer’s Truesoul label.


A name fans are used to hearing on the radar as a Drumcode artist, Nicole Moudaber shows us what she can do on the funkier side of the spectrum with her new EP landing on Truesoul. Both tracks merge her signature techno sound into something containing stacks of class and character. The evolving sounds build tension and energy to create the perfect after hour burners.


After a month of traveling from coast to coast including a busy Memorial Day Weekend at Mysteryland USA and Movement Detroit, Nicole Moudaber concludes her North American ‘Her Dub Material’ tour this month with appearances at Bestival Toronto and Spring Awakening Music Festival.


Nicole Moudaber ‘Somewhere In Our Minds’ EP is out June 8 exclusively on Beatport .


Nicole Moudaber plays the Bollywood Stage at Bestival Friday, June 12.
