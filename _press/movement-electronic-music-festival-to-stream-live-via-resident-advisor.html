FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

MOVEMENT ELECTRONIC MUSIC FESTIVAL IN DETROIT
MAY 28, 29, 30, 2011

MOVEMENT TO STREAM LIVE VIA RESIDENT ADVISOR



People across the world will be able to experience the sounds of the Movement Electronic Music Festival live as they are happening thanks to a new partnership Paxahau formed this year with Resident Advisor and Awdio.

On May 28, 29, and 30 from 12 noon until 12 midnight fans of electronic music will be able to visit www.ResidentAdvisor.net to access five media players being powered by the folks at www.Awdio.com to hear live performances from the vitaminwater, Red Bull Music Academy, Beatport, Made in Detroit and Movement Torino stages.  Fans will also be able to share media players with their friends on Facebook.

“Movement is an international festival with a global fan-base and this is an exciting new way for Paxahau to be able to share it with the fans that are unable to make it to Detroit,” said Jason Huvaere, director of operations, Movement Electronic Music Festival.  “In addition, we think the live streaming will help to expand the festival’s reach worldwide, and we are very happy to be partnering with Resident Advisor and Awdio to make it happen.”

Resident Advisor will broadcast all five (5) stages, across all three days on the following pages:

DAY ONE: www.residentadvisor.net/movement-day1
DAY TWO: www.residentadvisor.net/movement-day2
DAY THREE: www.residentadvisor.net/movement-day3

Resident Advisor is the world’s leading electronic music and club culture magazine and Awdio is the first platform dedicated to live music streaming that enables artists, producers, labels, clubs, venues, and promoters to stream their live music events in real-time, worldwide.

For more info about Movement and to purchase tickets, please go to: www.movement.us.

About Movement Electronic Music Festival
Movement Electronic Music Festival is an internationally recognized music marathon that delivers three days and three nights of non-stop performances from more than 100 artists across five stages set in an outdoor plaza located along the Detroit River.  Headlining talent from around the world performs alongside legendary hometown heroes as thousands of festival-goers dance to the music, and then keep the celebrations going at a full roster of epic after parties.  Nearly 100,000 people from across the globe attended the festival in 2010.  Weekend and VIP passes are now available along with the line-up at www.Movement.us.

About Resident Advisor
Resident Advisor (RA) is the world’s leading electronic music and club culture magazine.  Established in 2001, RA publishes news, reviews and features to an audience of more than a million monthly readers. Thousands of artist and label profiles—and even more user-generated event and club listings—have made RA an invaluable resource to both the clued-up music lover and the clubgoer simply looking for a good night out. With offices in London and Berlin - and shortly San Francisco - and contributors spanning the globe, RA covers anything and everything that is electronic music-related.  Visit www.ResidentAdvisor.net

About Awdio
Awdio is the first platform dedicated to live music streaming, enabling artists, producers, labels, clubs, venues, promoters, and all fine music purveyors to stream their live music events in real-time, worldwide. With over 150 venues & 100 artists already connected, Awdio broadcasts live quality DJ sets and live concerts 24/7.  Visit www.Awdio.com.

About Paxahau Promotions Group
Paxahau, now in its sixth year as producers of the Electronic Music Festival in Detroit, first established itself 13 years ago as an online resource to promote both Detroit and electronic music.  Paxahau has since earned the respect of their peers and formed a very strong network of alliances throughout the music industry.  Today, Paxahau Promotions Group is recognized across the world by artists and industry executives as a premier production and promotions organization.  To learn more, visitwww.paxahau.com.
