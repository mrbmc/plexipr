FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

PACHA MIAMI 2010
@ DREAM & PLAY AT NIGHT

World famous nightlife staple Pacha NYC brings some of its favorite talent to two of Miami’s favorite venues.  Hot spot nightclubs Dream and Play will serve as host to a slew of events brought to you by the one and only New York mega club.  First off, Play brings New York’s legendary Crooklyn Clan for a very special set from Pacha resident DJs DJ Riz & Sizzahandz, plus an exclusive performance from international hip hop superstar Kelis.  On Saturday, the club features some of South America’s finest grooves as Made in Columbia invades South Beach.  Dream brings the one and only Sharam to the decks on Thursday.  The DJ icon will be launching his new MACH series for the lucky Miami audience.  Then on Friday, one of Pacha NYC favorite parties Mind Control is back with Richie Santana and Peter Bailey and special guest Carlos Lio.  Finally, the legendary Star 69 label is set to close out the conference in style with Peter Rauhofer and Tom Stephan providing the final beats of another fun filled week.

For more info, please go to the Pacha Miami 2010 mini-site: www.pachanyc.com/miami2010/

Thursday, March 25th, 2010
The Crooklyn Clan & Moodswing 360 in association with Pacha NYC present
THE CROOKLYN CLAN PARTY
DJ RIZ & SIZZAHANDZ (Live on 4 Turntables!)
Special Performance by KELIS
along with
DISCOTECH, BEAT BREAKER, DANNY DIGGZ, SERAFIN, ROCK-IT-SCIENTISTS
Opening set by DJ MIMIC
$25 advance tickets
@ Play Nightclub

New York’s infamous Crooklyn Clan aka DJ Riz and Sizzahandz have been responsible for some the finest remixes, productions, and mash ups heard in clubs today.  Staple’s of the New York scene since the early 90’s, Crooklyn Clan is back in Miami for another year with a slew of very special guests including the one and only Kelis.

Thursday March 25th, 2010
Pacha NYC, Global Adrenaline, and Groove Cruise present
SHARAM: MACH 1
MACH Series Launch Party
$35 advance tickets
@ Dream Nightclub

What more needs to be said about house music icon Sharam that hasn’t already been said.  Having put together a resume that few within the scene can even begin to dream of, Sharam has seemingly done it all.  He is set to launch his new series Mach with an exclusive set at Miami hotspot Dream. His first new recorded output of 2010 in the form of 'Volume 1' of The Mach Series having just debuted on Pete Tong's Essential Selection, this is the first in a series of 3 EPs that will light up dance floors throughout the Spring leading to Sharam’s second studio album scheduled for release in September 2010.  Be sure to get a glimpse of the new website launching at www.sharam.com.

Friday, March 26th, 2010
Pacha NYC presents
MIND CONTROL
RICHIE SANTANA & PETER BAILEY
with special guest CARLOS LIO
$35 advance tickets
@ Dream Nightclub

New York’s purveyors of hard, twisted tribal beats Richie Santana and Peter Bailey hold one of Pacha NYC’s most beloved events.  Their Mind Control events have filled clubs and rocked dance floors around the country and they bring the event back to Dream for another year of techno insanity

Saturday, March 27th, 2010
Pacha NYC presents
MADE IN COLUMBIA
MARIO OCHOA
ALEX POXADA, IVAN D, NIKOLAS
$30 advance tickets
@ Play Nightclub

The sounds of South America land on the shores of Miami as Made in Columbia invades South Beach.  Expect some of house music’s best beats with a distinct Latin flavor at this event.

Sunday, March 28th, 2010
Pacha NYC presents
STAR 69 RECORDS MIAMI 2010 CLOSING PARTY
PETER RAUHOFER & TOM STEPHAN AKA SUPERCHUMBO
$20 advance tickets
@ Dream Nightclub

Close out another week of musical debauchery in style as the legendary Star 69 records brings the final beats of conference with two DJ legends.  Peter Rauhofer has been a perennial favorite for nearly two decades, performing around the world and producing and remixing some of the finest tracks out there.  Rauhofer is joined by another favorite, Tom Stephan aka Superchumbo as they are set to leave the lasting memory in the minds of Miami party goers.
