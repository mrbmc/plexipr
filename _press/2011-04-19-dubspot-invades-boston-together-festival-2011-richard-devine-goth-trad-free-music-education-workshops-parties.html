FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE


Dubspot Invades Boston!
Together Festival 2011 - Richard Devine, Goth Trad, Robotkid, XI, Nalepa, Shadetek, Endo
Free Music Education Workshops & Parties

Surefire Sound North American Tour with Ramadanman & Zeb Bias Presented By Dubspot
Road Warrior Tour with Mary Anne Hobbs US Tour Presented by Dubspot

Dubspot is excited to join Boston’s Together Festival for the first time, hosting a wide range of events, from production and performance workshops, to panel discussions, to live performances, parties, and benefits.

Dubspot kicks off on Wednesday, April 20 at Good Life with Ramadanman and Zed Bias. Thursday, April 21 and Friday, April 22 there will be a series of free Dubspot Hi-Fidelity Sessions at Boston’s Bristol Studios with Richard Devine, Goth Trad, Steve Nalepa and other Dubspot instructors, plus a special performance from Richard Devine on Friday night. On Saturday, April 23, from 12noon - 6pm, Dubspot will host a series of demo sessions at the Together Festival “Get Together” Trade Show & Expo. Wrapping things up properly on Sunday, April 24 will be the Dubspot-sponsored Road Warrior Tour featuring Mary Anne Hobbs, Lorn, Gonjasufi, Take, and Strangeloop at Boston’s Paradise Rock Club. See a full listing of Dubspot activities events at Together Festival 2011 below!

Thursday, April 21 –  Friday, April 22
The Dubspot Hi-Fi Sessions at Together Festival, Boston 2011
Artist Workshops Featuring: Richard Devine, Goth Trad, XI, Matt Shadetek, Steve Nalepa, Duncan Laurie, Robotkid, DJ Endo, and some very special guests
1:00pm – 8:00pm @ Bristol Studios (169 Massachusetts Ave)
Free to the public

The Dubspot Hi-Fidelity Sessions represent what Dubspot aspires to bring the public: unique and exciting ways for uniting the electronic music scene and educating the people within it. At each stop, a select group of world-class DJs, producers, sound designers, and musicians join top-notch Dubspot instructors to present production and performance strategies in an intimate workshop environment.

Friday, April 22
Dubspot & Bassic present Together Japan with Goth Trad, XI, Dev/Null, and Richard Devine
9:00pm - 2:00am @ Good Life (28 Kingston St.)
$7 before 11pm / $10 after

A unique producer with a unique style, Goth-Trad (of Rebel Familia) has emerged from the Japanese electronic scene in the last few years as one of the most arresting artists from his generation. Freely playing samplers, keyboards and even self-made instruments, ‘The Sound Originator’ Goth-Trad creates remarkable dance music with an abstract approach. Toronto’s XI has quickly become a rising star in the city’s electronic music scene. His eclectic taste and approach to bass-driven beats has landed him on the bill with Prodigy LIVE and Pendulum LIVE, as well as headlining slots across Canada & the USA. In terms of production, XI already has a number of releases under his belt on both the solo and collaborative tip including work with Dub War’s Juakali, fellow Canadian producer DZ and tracks under the Loetech moniker alongside friend and Toronto producer Bombaman, to name a few.
