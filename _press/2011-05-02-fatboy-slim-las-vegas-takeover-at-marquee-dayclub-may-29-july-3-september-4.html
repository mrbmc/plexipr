FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE


MARQUEE DAYCLUB AT THE COSMOPOLITAN OF LAS VEGAS
FATBOY SLIM LAS VEGAS TAKE OVER
May 29 | July 3 | September 4

With Las Vegas firmly established as the newest global dance music capital, Marquee Dayclub presents Fatboy Slim -- one of the most iconic and fearless dance music ringleaders -- to take over Sin City on Sunday, May 29 for an afternoon of unbridled disco deviance during its ‘United We Play’ Memorial Day Weekend extravaganza. TheFatboy Slim Las Vegas Takeover continues this summer on ensuing holiday weekends: July 3 and September 4.

Hailing from Brighton, UK, Fatboy Slim is no stranger to an enviable tradition of wild hedonism, abandon and iconic parties. So it’s no coincidence that he’s bringing the spirit and decadence of his now legendary ‘Big Beach Boutique’ club to the party capital of the world. Expect the full Fatboy Slim DJ experience at Marquee: remixes and re-edits of bouncy house tracks and irrepressible big-beats that grab you by the hips and pull you head first into a dancefloor full of flailing arms and legs. What other town could do justice to a party like this but Sin City? Following in the tradition of boundary-pushing Vegas pioneers like Siegfried & Roy, Liberace and Barbara Streisand, Fatboy Slim kicks off the summer holiday season this Memorial Day Weekend and commands, “As your attorney I advise you to attend...Fatboy Slim’s Las Vegas Take Over!”

Always exuding an unmatched passion and ear for rhythm, Fatboy Slim is responsible for chart-topping, genre-busting club anthems like “Praise You,” “The Rockafeller Skank” and “Right Here Right Now” and era-defining moments such as drawing 250,000 people to his performance on Brighton Beach. He is best known for his number one albums ‘You've Come Along Way Baby’ and ‘Half Way Between The Gutter and The Stars,’ and he has collaborated with artists including Blur, David Byrne, The Beastie Boys, Florence Welch and Santigold. A music video pioneer, his “‘Praise You” video is the number one MTV video of all time, and no one will ever forget his partnership with Spike Jonze and Christopher Walken on the iconic video “Weapon Of Choice.” His recent world tours have seen him sell out stadiums in Asia, Africa and Europe, lead the procession at Rio’s Carnival, and headline the world’s biggest festivals, from Glastonbury to Global Gathering.

Fatboy Slim joins a superstar DJ lineup -- including Armin van Buuren, Laidback Luke, Chuckie and many more -- for Marquee’s ‘United We Play’ Memorial Day Weekend extravaganza, May 27 to 30, featuring Nightclub events and daytime parties at its lavish, sun-drenched, newly opened Dayclub, complete with several bars, two pools, eight cabanas, exclusive bungalows, specialty cocktails and food menu.
