This Independence Day weekend, Pacha NYC celebrates a weekend full of its newest DJ residencies for Danny Tenaglia and Chus & Ceballos, plus the return of Subliminal Records head honcho and original Pacha resident Erick Morillo, on a quick jaunt back from the mothership Pacha Ibiza. The legendary Tenaglia will ignite the dancefloor fireworks on Thursday, July 2nd with Erick Morillo carrying the torch on Friday, July 3rd, then Madrid's Chus & Ceballos to close-out the mega weekend with a bang on Saturday July 4th.

Pacha NYC is also proud to announce the addition of Italian electro-house maestro Benny Benassi, set to deliver satisfaction to his adoring NYC crowds beginning January 2010 as its newest resident DJ.  Benassi now joins the elite roster of Pacha NYC resident DJs including; Benny Benassi, Boris, Chus & Ceballos, Danny Tenaglia, Erick Morillo, Jonathan Peters and Victor Calderone.

Get ready for a hot summer season at Pacha NYC with its globally-acclaimed DJ residents and special guests!

Danny Tenaglia ignites his new monthly residency at Pacha NYC on Thursday, July 2nd, 2009.  After the end of his beloved “Be Yourself” residency at Vinyl/Arc (RIP 2004), Danny clearly waited for the right time, right place to launch another historical DJ residency at Pacha NYC, the last big-room standing with unparallel sound. It's no easy task to be labeled as "The DJ's DJ," but then again Danny Tenaglia is no ordinary DJ. At the pinnacle of his long-standing career, Danny is a DJ highly respected by his peers; both locally and internationally, and a performer with the uncanny ability to connect to the crowd like no other. He boasts an extensive record collection, as well as the Midas touch; able to drop just the right track or edit at just the right moment.

Beginning this July, find the freedom to be yourself when NYC will once again be an international showcase for one of the world’s greatest living DJs, as we continue our journey on the dancefloor each ensuing month. Get ready for the next chapter in NYC’s storied dance music history!

Subliminal Records main man Erick Morillo takes a quick break from Ibiza to return to his own reigning Pacha NYC residency on Friday, July 3rd, 2009. This summer he will also showcase the hottest tunes on the Subliminal Sessions Summer 2009 album due out in August 2009. The album will showcase tracks from Who Da Funk, Mark Brown & Juan Kid, Dean Newton & Huggy Ministers, De La Fun feat. Duane Harden and Erick Morillo feat. Deborah Cooper. Be Bad, the theme for this summer’s Subliminal parties at flagship Pacha Ibiza will surely bring out the badness out of this legendary DJ/producer that will burn dance floors from New York to Ibiza this summer.

Madrid-based DJ/producers Chus & Ceballos launch their brand new DJ residency at Pacha NYC starting Saturday, July 4th, 2009. The duo are behind the globally renown Stereo Productions label and the iconic “Iberican Sound”; rhythmic, percussive electronic house with a touch of soul storming from the Iberican peninsula. They have been driving the musical motorship on the house front captivating dancefloors throughout the world via massive and lively list of remixes such as Luzon “The Baguio Track”, Deep Dish “Say Hello” and Madonna’s “Hung Up” have gained them tremendous reputation in the music industry. They hold new exclusive residencies this summer at Pacha Ibiza, Flamingo Night with Fedde Le Grand, Pure Pacha Closing Party with Sander Kleinenberg and this July 4th at Pacha NYC. Their remixes and original productions are long awaited with huge expectation as they are the guiding light for a huge group of artists, DJs, producers and fans. This year Chus & Ceballos will release “Iberican Sound 2009 Mixes” under their label Stereo Productions. Also look out for their remix of Oscar G feat. Tamara Wallace- “Back To You” under Nervous Records.
