Hi there,
 
See below for a list of Plexi PR’s client events at Miami Music Week with Apollonia, Joseph Capriati, Nicole Moudaber & composer Ricardo Romaneiro. Please contact us for media guestlist and artist interview requests!  

Thanks and have a great weekend!

Lydia

APOLLONIA
 

  • French super-trio is composed of Dan Ghenacia, Dyed Soundorom and Shonky
  • Tour à Tour album remixes are set to be released in three EPs with reworks from Charles Webster, Edward, Hold Youth, Jovonn Berlin, Mike Shannon, Terence:Terry, The Martinez Brothers, and Thomas Melchior.


Thursday, March 17 - Marco Carola vs. Apollonia @ Space Terrace
  • A legendary session on the Space Terrace with Apollonia b2b with Marco Carola going well into the next day. Advance tickets available on wantickets while supplies last.
  • For media guestlist email lydia@plexipr.com
Friday, March 18 - Apollonia @ Ultra Carl Cox & Friends Stage 4:00pm-6:30pm

 

JOSEPH CAPRIATI

  • Italian techno titan Joseph Capriati is set to launch his own record label REDIMENSION later this year.

Monday, March 14 - Joseph Capriati presents 100% JC @ Trade
  • For his 100% JC concept nights, Joseph Capriati dedicates himself to longer extended sets featuring specially created original edits and remixes. Advance tickets available on Resident Advisor while supplies last.
  • For media guestlist email lydia@plexipr.com
Friday, March 18 - Joseph Capriati @ Ultra Carl Cox & Friends Stage 6:30pm-8:00pm

 

NICOLE MOUDABER

  • Nicole Moudaber’s upcoming ‘Where Shadows Lie’ EP is out on Carl Cox and Jon Rundell’s revered underground imprint Intec Digital March 14 - Check out "Old Soul 'Young But Not New”’ here.

Thursday, March 17 - Nicole Moudaber MoodDAY @ Raleigh Hotel Pool with Carl Craig, Damian Lazarus, ENDO, Guti (live) and Pan-Pot
  • Nicole Moudaber returns to Miami for her annual MoodDAY pool party where she’ll be celebrating the 100th episode of her In The MOOD Radio show with a live global broadcast. Advance tickets available at bit.ly/MoodDAY2016 while supplies last.
  • For media guestlist email stephanie@plexipr.com
Saturday, March 19 - Nicole Moudaber @ Ultra Carl Cox & Friends Stage 4:00pm-6:00pm

Saturday, March 19 - Nicole Moudaber MoodNIGHT @ Trade all night long
  • For those yearning for more of Nicole’s deep and dark techno, journey late into the night as she conducts an open-to-close set at her MoodNIGHT party. Advance tickets available at bit.ly/MoodNIGHT2016 while supplies last.
  • For media guestlist email stephanie@plexipr.com

 
RICARDO ROMANEIRO


Friday, March 18 - Ricardo Romaneiro @ Pairings at Ted’s (with after-hours DJ set by Kate Simko)
Saturday, March 19 - Ricardo Romaneiro @ Pairings at Ted’s

  • Join the genre-bending 21st century orchestra Nu Deco Ensemble perform Ricardo Romaneiro’s electro-acoustic ambient work : Synthetic Mosaic : (adapted for clarinet, percussion, keyboard, viola and electronics), for an evening at Miami's latest artistic supper club-inspired series that pairs world-class performances with a themed culinary experience.  
  • Following the Nu Disco Ensemble performance on Friday, March 18, stay for a late night DJ set by prolific electronic music producer and film composer Kate Simko.
  • Advance tickets available at www.youngarts.org/teds while supplies last.
  • For media guestlist email stephanie@plexipr.com
