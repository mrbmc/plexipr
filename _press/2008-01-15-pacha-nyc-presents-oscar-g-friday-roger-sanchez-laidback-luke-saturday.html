Friday, January 18th
Pacha NYC presents
OSCAR G
Returns to NYC

Murk member and Miami resident Oscar G returns to Pacha, bearing the dark drums, adventurous electro and hot-chick vocals for which he's known. Show up early for free music from his unreleased new album, INNOV8. New York locals Vinyl Life open up. In the basement, Bassmint Sesions with VTone. Hip Hop & Mashups in the Funky Room with special guest DJ Odi Roc featuring the Pacha Shower Dancers.

Saturday, January 19th
Pacha NYC presents
ROGER SANCHEZ
along with special guest
upcoming producer of the moment
LAIDBACK LUKE

If you love house, this is your night. Dance legend Roger Sanchez brings his Release Yourself party back to Pacha with a special guest, international mystery man Laidback Luke. GRAMMY winner Sanchez has been pounding global dancefloors all year, snagging a Club World Award 2008 "Best DJ Set" nomination for his October Miami stop. And Philippines-born, Dutch-raised Luke is fresh off collaborations with the hit-making Swedish House Mafia.

Celebrating the release of their new mix CD AFTERDARK VOL. 3 out now on Stealth Records.
