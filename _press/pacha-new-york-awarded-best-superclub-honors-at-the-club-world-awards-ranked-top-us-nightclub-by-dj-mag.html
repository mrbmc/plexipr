Pacha New York Awarded Best Superclub
Honors At The Club World Awards
Also Ranked Top U.S. Nightclub by DJ Mag

Amidst the frenzy of parties and celebrations at the Winter Music Conference this year, Pacha NYC has come out on top again, winning ‘Best Superclub' for the 2nd consecutive year at The 6th Annual Club World Awards, beating out tough competition from Hollywood, Vegas, Miami, and Toronto. Nominated in seven categories, Pacha NYC received a total of three of the coveted Disco Globe awards including, ‘Best Resident LJ' and ‘Best DJ Set' by Danny Tenaglia for his amazing DJ performance at the Vinyl Reunion party.

After the prestigious awards show, Eddie Dean co-owner of Pacha NYC exuded, "We're proud of our accomplishments to date and all year-round our staff strives for excellence at every turn. To be recognized by our fellow industry peers is truly an honor and this is something that will be shared by our management, staff, DJs, promoters and every single person who contributes to our ongoing success. We are truly grateful for this award but we promise to continue to present the best clubbing experience forward and live up to our being named the Best Superclub." He continued on to talk about future expansion plans, "Now we want to continue to explore our options in Las Vegas and Miami to bring the Pacha brand of excellence in nightlife across North America."

In DJ Mag's latest issue, the revered clubbing bible ranks the Top 100 clubs in 2008 worldwide. The magazine approached the world's biggest DJs and asked them to vote for their top three venues in the world, namely the clubs that have left a lasting impression on them for the last 12 months. With Pacha Ibiza leading the charge at #7, Pacha New York came in at #24, the highest ranking U.S. nightclub in the survey.
Over the last 2 years, Pacha NYC has risen to the top of North America's clubbing capital and has become a staple in NYC's nightlife, by attracting the most sought after global and NYC DJs with an international, cosmopolitan clubbing clientele. Week after week Pacha NYC delivers the very best clubbing nights with the venue's over-the-top debauchery, performances, spectacular sound, quality production and dazzling lighting.

About Pacha New York
Pacha New York is the newest member of the international nightlife consortium and the first North American branch of the club. Pacha, the international brand was founded by Ricardo Urgell in 1967 with a single club in Sitges, Spain and the family-run business has since evolved into a global lifestyle brand that encompasses more than 21 nightclubs, a hotel, restaurants, a record label, radio station, lifestyle magazine and a full-scale merchandising division. Since its opening in December 2005, the 30,000 square feet club has won numerous awards and distinctions including: Club World Awards 2008 awards for - Best Superclub, Best Resident LJ and Best DJ Set: Danny Tenaglia; Club World Awards 2007 - Best Superclub, Best New Venue, Best Soundsystem; DJ Mag Top US Club of the World 2008; Best Of Citysearch: New York Nightlife 2007 #1 Best Dance Club; AOL City's Best 2007 - Best Dance Club; and ‘Top 10 Hottest Megaclub in America' by Maxim magazine.
