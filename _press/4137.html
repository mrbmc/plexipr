Announces SKRILLEX

General Admission | VIP & Platinum Experience
3 Day Passes Available Now


Skrillex at Electric Zoo 2011. Photo credit: Jordan Loyd for ElectricZooFestival.com

Electric Zoo: New York’s Electronic Music Festival is proud to announce the addition of Skrillex to the Main Stage on Sunday, September 2. The multiple Grammy-winning star joins a stellar lineup of the world’s best electronic music artists playing over three days on Randall’s Island Park, New York City, on August 31, September 1 and September 2, 2012.

Since his epic set at Electric Zoo 2011, Skrillex has gone on to sell-out a massive Mothership Tour across North America, take over clubs in Los Angeles and New York, and was nominated for five Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist. With three Grammys under his belt for his chart-topping ‘Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites’ EP and remix of “Cinema” by Benny Benassi feat. Gary Go, Skrillex dropped his ‘Bangarang’ EP in December, featuring collaborations with The Doors, Wolfgang Gartner, 12th Planet, Kill The Noise and Ellie Goulding. Skrillex returns to Randall's Island, this time closing out Electric Zoo 2012.

For more info, please go to www.electriczoofestival.com.

About Electric Zoo
Electric Zoo: New York’s Electronic Music Festival now in its 4th year, featured more than 100 of the world’s biggest electronic music talent in 2011, including DJs and live acts, providing the finest techno, trance, house, dubstep, electro, indie dance music and more across four stages on beautiful Randall’s Island Park. Electric Zoo received a coveted IDMA nomination for “Best U.S. Festival” and “Best Music Event” by URB magazine, as well as press coverage from major media outlets like CNN, The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Huffington Post, Village Voice, Time Out New York, New York Post, The Source, DJ Mag, Mixmag and Ocean Drive, establishing it as New York City’s premier electronic music festival as well as a top American event. After a sold-out capacity crowd of 50,000 over 2 days in 2010, Electric Zoo expanded to a new 3-day format with over 85,000 attendees in 2011.
