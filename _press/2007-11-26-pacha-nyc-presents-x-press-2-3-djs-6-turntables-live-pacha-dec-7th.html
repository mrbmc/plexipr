Makeshift Feelgood CD Release Party

Pacha NYC presents X-PRESS 2 Live on Saturday, December 7th. The three DJs are back in the US to celebrate the release of their new album MAKESHIFT FEELGOOD out now on Tommy Boy Records.

For your foot-tapping pleasure and mind-expanding enjoyment, we present the men of X-Press 2: Rocky, Diesel and Ashley Beedle. The UK club-rockers are back with Makeshift Feelgood, the follow-up to the 100,000 selling Muzikizum. Their latest album features lead vocals by Tim DeLaughter (The Polyphonic Spree), Kurt Wagner (Lambchop), Anthony Roman (Radio 4), Rob Harvey (The Music), Kissing The Pink and Bernard Fowler (NYC Peech Boys / Rolling Stones).

The last time they were stateside, their humongous fanbase propelled their vinyl-only 12-inches (i.e.: "London X-Press," "Muzik X-Press," "The Sound") - sold sans barcodes- thumping up the charts. But they've been away for a while, sweating in the DJ trenches, frequenting international clubs aplenty, trapped in the studio with Sixties sunshine-pop LPs, wrangling vocal stars from far and wide. But four years after they-and The Talking Heads' David Byrne-swaggered to #2 on the UK pop charts) in the charts with "Lazy," then waltzed home with an Ivor Novello Award, the X-Press 2 triumvirate is back!

X-PRESS 2 - MAKESHIFT FEELGOOD is out now on Tommy Boy Records.
