ELECTRIC ZOO ADDS AXWELL TO HEADLINING TALENT


New York’s Electronic Music Festival
September 4 & 5, 2010 (Labor Day Weekend)
Randall's Island Park

Open Air Festival 11am-11pm Each Day
70+ Artists Over Multiple Stages

The countdown has officially begun; Electric Zoo, New York's Electronic Music Festival is now only one month away!  This Labor Day weekend, September 4th  & 5th , 2010, over 70 of the world’s best representatives of electronic music from around the globe will descend on the Big Apple for a holiday weekend full of great music, top notch production and impeccable sound.   This amazing lineup features a who’s who of electronic music heavyweights and legends gathered together for this one-of-a-kind spectacular event.

Due to phenomenal demand, we are pleased to announce the latest addition of superstar headlining talent.  You asked for him and we listened; now the Swedish House Mafia’s own AXWELL will bring his talents to the Hilltop Arena on Saturday, September 4th. Voted "Best House Artist" in Beatport Music Awards 2010, this DJ, remixer, record producer and record label owner from Sweden, maintains a busy international touring schedule, as well as a successful summer residency at Pacha Ibiza. Axwell’s latest single “Nothing But Love” is climbing the top 10 chart at Beatport and the Swedish House Mafia’s massive debut single “One (Your Name) feat Pharrell” is a bona fide club anthem of the summer. Their forthcoming, highly anticipated debut artist album - Swedish House Mafia 'Until One' is out this October 2010.

About Electric Zoo
After its inaugural year, Electric Zoo has already established itself as New York’s premier electronic music festival.  In 2009, 26,000 people passed through the gates of Randall’s Island Park to witness over 50 acts representing multiple countries on 4 stages.  Electric Zoo represents all genres of electronic music by bringing top international DJs and live acts along with hometown favorites to New York City. The festival’s inaugural edition received a coveted IDMA nomination for “Best U.S. Festival” and “Best Music Event” by URB magazine, as well as positive press coverage from major media outlets like CNN, New York Times, Huffington Post, Village Voice, Time Out New York, New York Post, DJ Mag, and Ocean Drive.

About Made Event
Made Event, helmed by Mike Bindra and Laura De Palma, working with a team of industry leaders, is committed to excellence in the programming, promotion, and production of electronic dance music events. They stand at the vanguard of their field due to the world-renowned caliber of entertainment and expert production values they bring into a variety of distinctive and innovative venues. In 2009, Made Event was nominated for “Promoter of the Year” by URB magazine and “Best Event Promoter” by the IDMAs. With over 25 years of combined experience, and over 400 successful events produced in New York City and at Winter Music Conference in Miami, Made Event continues to set the bar for quality electronic music events.  For more info, please go to www.MadeEvent.com.
