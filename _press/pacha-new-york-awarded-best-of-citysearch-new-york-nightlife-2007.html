PACHA NEW YORK AWARDED

BEST OF CITYSEARCH: NEW YORK
NIGHTLIFE 2007

#1 Best Dance Club

#1 Best Singles Scene

#1 Best See and Be Seen Bar

#3 Best Bartenders

Manhattan hotspot Pacha was awarded the #1 spot in the Best of Citysearch: New York Nightlife 2007 poll in the categories of Best Dance Club, Best Singles Scene, Best See and Be Seen Bar and the #3 spot for Best Bartenders. These designations were all Audience winners based on public online voting.Co-owner Eddie Dean states, "We're pleased to be recognized by Citysearch and the voting public. It affirms that we are providing our guests with the utmost professionalism and giving them the premiere clubbing experience in line with the international Pacha brand." He continues, "We look forward to many more years of service to the public as we explore further options to expand the Pacha standard of clubbing to other cities in North America."

Pacha's increasing popularity and standing as the premier nightclub in NYC is further cemented by the Citysearch rankings. Now with upwards of half a million patrons coming through our doors this year and 80,000 visits to the Pacha website (www.pachanyc.com) each month, Pacha continues to provide the best nightlife experience for a cosmopolitan global audience.

Since it opened doors in December 2005, Pacha New York has brought a world-class level of nightlife to New York City, along with an international roster of world class DJs including co-owner and resident Erick Morillo, Carl Cox, Danny Tenaglia, Pacha NYC residents Jonathan Peters, Boris, Victor Calderone, James Zabiela, and the world's #1 DJ Armin van Buuren (DJ Mag). Pacha has quickly established itself as a nightlife fixture with accolades including being named "Top 10 Hottest Megaclub" in America by Maxim Magazine, "#1 Best Dance Club" by AOL CityGuide New York City's Best Nightlife poll and receiving numerous Club World Awards in 2007 for "Best Superclub", "Best New Venue" and "Best Sound System". From an international outlook, Pacha New York has been included in the DJ Mag Top 50 Clubs of the World, entering the list as a new entry at #20 and the highest-ranking New York City club.

ABOUT PACHA

Pacha New York is the newest member of the international nightlife consortium and the first North American branch of the club. Pacha, the international brand was founded by Ricardo Urgell in 1967 with a single club in Sitges, Spain and the family-run business has since evolved into a global lifestyle brand that encompasses more than 24 nightclubs, a hotel, restaurants, a record label, radio station, lifestyle magazine and a full-scale merchandising division. With over 30,000 square feet and four distinct levels, Pacha New York is the ideal space for hosting an intimate event, a corporate party or a colossal celebration.
