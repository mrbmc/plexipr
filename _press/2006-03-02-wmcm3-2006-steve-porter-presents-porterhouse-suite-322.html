Steve Porter\'s new album PORTERHOUSE hits the streets today in anticipation of his annual Winter Music Conference Porterhouse party - this year to be held as a kick-off to the conference on Wednesday, March 22 @ Suite with elaborate decorations, live visuals, dancers and all the Porterhouse bells and whistles to make this the standout event of WMC 2006.

Steve Porter releases his first-ever mix compilation PORTERHOUSE with 26 tracks melded together in his signature genre-defying, pulsating, energetic Porterhouse style. The album merges his DJing and production style with 9 of his own original tracks, further blurring the distinction between DJ and producer into one compelling artist to watch.
