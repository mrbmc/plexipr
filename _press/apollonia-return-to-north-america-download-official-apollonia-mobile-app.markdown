<div style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">French super-trio <b>Apollonia</b>, composed of </span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; color: #222222; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap; background-color: #ffffff;"><b>Dan Ghenacia, Dyed Soundorom</b> and <b>Shonky</b>,</span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"> power into the fall and winter season with dates across North America -- including the celebrated <i>Time Warp US</i> in New York on November 21-- and unveil their brand new mobile app, featuring exclusive interactive content including music, news, interviews, mixtapes, and more. </span></div>
<p></p>
<div style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Available for free download for Android </span><a style="text-decoration: none;" href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.goodbarber.apollonia"><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; color: #1155cc; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap; text-decoration: underline;">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"> and Apple iOS </span><a style="text-decoration: none;" href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/apollonia/id930927805?mt=8"><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; color: #1155cc; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap; text-decoration: underline;">here</span></a><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">, the official Apollonia app allows fans to follow the globetrotting trio as they take their amazing three-way back-to-back-to-back performances around the world. Interact with Dyed, Shonky and Dan, catch up with all their latest news and interviews, discover personal guides to their favorite cities, and have some fun with a game featuring the guys themselves. Meet the artists who have become part of Apollonia&rsquo;s label family, get a hold of releases early, and gain exclusive access to hard-to-find mixtapes, the latest of which is </span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; font-style: italic; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">BBC Radio 1&rsquo;s Pete Tong&rsquo;s MBE presents Apollonia Sound of Ibiza</span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></div>
<p></p>

</span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; font-style: italic; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Circoloco at DC10</span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">, </span><span style="font-size: 13.3333px;
