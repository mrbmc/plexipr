Nicole Moudaber & Friends
‘BREED’ EP Official Release Party - Saturday, September 12
with Victor Calderone, Joel Mull, Hector and Lauren Lane

**New Location: The Brooklyn Mirage**
99 Scott Ave. Brooklyn, NY

[YouTube] Nicole Moudaber & Skin "Someone Like You"-- Watch here


 

Before New York’s summer fizzles out, MOOD Records label head Nicole Moudaber will heat things up with an official release party for her upcoming ‘BREED’ EP at her Nicole Moudaber & Friends showcase this Saturday, September 12. Due to overwhelming demand the event has been moved from Coney Island to the most buzzed about New York venue this summer, The Brooklyn Mirage, located at 99 Scott Ave. Brooklyn, NY.


Created by Reynard Productions, The Brooklyn Mirage is a new outdoor and indoor event space in East Williamsburg open only this summer, July through September. With expansive KV2 Audio to immerse attendees in crystalline sound, meticulously designed to create a sense of warmth and intimacy, platforms and levels for viewing Brooklyn and the city, an indoor lounge for breaks during the day, fine dine and drink, creature comforts and so many other details left to surprise, it is truly a Mirage: now you see the dream, at summer's end you won't.


The open air show will feature a slew of talent from techno's major players with Nicole Moudaber performing back-to-back with Victor Calderone along with sets from Swedish connoisseur Joel Mull, Vatos Locos don Hector, New York native Lauren Lane and a special warm up set from Nicole Moudaber!
Nicole is dedicating this RPM Presents show to the ‘HOUSE OF ROB,’ the legacy left behind by NYC nightlife icon and legend Rob Fernandez. Rob was instrumental in introducing Nicole to the NYC stage and championed her as he often did for artists that he believed in. His legacy and genius live on in each party and every beat on the dancefloor. Nicole will donate a portion of the proceeds to the Fernandez Family.
Nicole states: “I'm having this show with a lot of sadness in my heart, but also knowing that Rob is above me with a smile. I decided to dedicate this event to him because of the circumstances. I wasn't sure the event was going to happen when Rob left us but RPM wanted to go ahead with it. It is a very special one. It's my first festival and it’s in New York City and he won't be there to see it. The only idea that keeps me going is that he would have wanted it to happen. And I want to do everything to channel all the energy on that special day, for Rob.”
Ambassador of dark and driving techno, Nicole Moudaber has teamed up with Skin, the multi-talented Skunk Anansie frontwoman, established DJ and fashion icon, for a collaborative EP entitled ‘BREED’. Two legendary artists in their own right, Moudaber and Skin have brought together cutting-edge creative and visual aesthetics to ensure ‘BREED’ is an assault on the senses. The exceptional songwriting and performance abilities of Skin coupled with Moudaber’s production finesse both point to ‘BREED’ being one of the most celebrated conceptual projects in techno. ‘BREED’ is out September 18 on MOOD Records. Following the launch of the video for their first single "You Like This" Nicole Moudaber & Skin have released their second track of their double single release, “Someone Like You” both available now on Beatport.
