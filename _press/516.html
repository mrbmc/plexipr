FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Friday, April 25th, 2008
Made Event presents
16 Bit Lolitas
(Hope, CR2 Records, Shinichi)
w/ Willie Graff

Made Event presents 16 Bit Lolitas' debut at award winning 'Best Club' Cielo on Friday, April 25th.

Over the years, 16 Bit Lolitas' Peter Kriek and Ariaan Olieroock have played at some of the largest festivals and club venues both here and abroad. Their tightly, well-crafted productions have found notoriety on dancefloors worldwide with releases across Yoshitoshi, Alternative Route, Release Records, Sadie Records, Plastic Fantastic, Nu Republic, Armada, Minimal records, DK records, and LMR. 16 Bit Lolita are now armed  with a new single on the horizon, "Single Baby You/Metaad01" due out on Hope Recordings on June 23rd and a brand, new mix album for Brazil's premier cub "Warung" coming out in Fall 2008.

Peter Kriek and Ariaan Olieroock had been individually making music for a while before they got together to become 16 Bit Lolitas in 2003. It was only till they moved from New York City to Amsterdam that they embarked on quest to do something new, creating a mature dance sound. Adding to Peter's creative genius, Ariaan exhibits a strong technical background and amazing DJ abilities--Both Peter and Ariaan share a deep-rooted passion for music, and with this they continue to grow as both producers and DJs.
