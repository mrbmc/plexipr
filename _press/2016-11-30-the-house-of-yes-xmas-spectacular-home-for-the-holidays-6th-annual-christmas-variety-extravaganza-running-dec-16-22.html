The House of Yes Xmas Spectacular: Home For The Holidays*

Xmas Spectacular at House of Yes 2015 | Photo Credit: Todd Seelie

This holiday season, Bushwick’s incredible performance space House of Yes once again welcomes back a special edition of its sixth annual Xmas Spectacular: Home For The Holidays, an immersive engagement of six performances running from December 16-22. Starring Anya Sapozhnikova and Kae Burke as themselves; the co-founders and co-creative directors of House of Yes, alongside a cast of 22 talented performers specializing in aerial, circus, dancing and mind boggling theatrics, the House of Yes Xmas Spectacular is set to be a holiday theatrical extravaganza like no other.

The Xmas Spectacular will follow House of Yes heroines Anya and Kae as they try to make it back to their home planet for Christmas before getting lost in an intergalactic void. Much like Dorothy’s adventures on the yellow brick road, Anya and Kae find themselves hitchhiking across the galaxy trying to make it home in a meteorite blizzard, come face to face with festive characters throughout their journey including: Misfit Drag Queens, Reindeer and Pain-deer, Venus Fly Traps, Santercize Instructors, North Pole Dancers, an alien Father Christmas, Santa Klaus Nomi, Baby Jesus herself and plenty more unearthly surprises. Featuring a variety of jaw-dropping aerial extravaganzas, an original live score mashed up with Christmas classics like Mariah Carey’s “All I Want For Christmas Is You,” choreographed dance numbers and choirs, this adult Christmas show is raunchy, absurd and hilarious in a way that only a House of Yes performance can be.

Complementing the full scale production, ticket holders will also be able to shop at the outer-space gift shop whilst sipping and nibbling on egg-nog and cookies, get their picture taken on Santa’s lap at the Holiday Photo Booth, and take in immersive intermission entertainment. For those wanting to get closer to the action and snag one of the 25 front row reserved seats up for grabs whilst indulging in a specialty House of Yes Christmas Cheers Elixir, VIP passes are available for purchase through Eventbrite. All Home For The Holidays ticket holders are welcome to stick around after the show and gain automatic entry to the following event that night.

Advance general admission tickets $35 and VIP passes $50 and more information available on Eventbrite.

About House of Yes:
House of Yes opened its third iteration in Bushwick at the beginning of 2016 to become a beacon of NYC nightlife after shuttering its doors too soon in East Williamsburg three years ago. Original partners Anya Sapozhnikova and Kae Burke, teamed up with Ilan Telmont and Justin Ahiyon to build out the best venue ever with a little help from their friends, seeding a Kickstarter campaign for $92,340 (original goal: $60,000). The space includes an art-deco restaurant parlor, a spectacularly decorated warehouse-sized performance space, a courtyard with kitschy repurposed decor, and a clandestine mini-club. House of Yes is now the home of circus spectacles, immersive cinema, burlesque and cabaret shows, aerial extravaganzas, nightlife parties, morning raves, brunch, BBQs, and bubble baths, all with superior sound and exemplary service. Each night is specially curated to create an immersive experience with music, theatrics, and performances to titillate the senses and expand your mind, unlike any other venue in Brooklyn right now.
