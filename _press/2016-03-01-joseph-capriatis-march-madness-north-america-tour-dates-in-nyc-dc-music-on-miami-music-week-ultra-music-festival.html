Making his mark on the global techno scene, Italian techno titan Joseph Capriati continues to drive his momentum to new heights in 2016. An established key artist for Adam Beyer’s Drumcode since 2009, a resident of Marco Carola’s Music On in Ibiza and a headlining DJ globally at renowned festivals such as Time Warp, Awakenings, Movement, The BPM Festival and Ultra Music Festival, the charismatic DJ looks to take on a new role as label head through his newly announced record label REDIMENSION, launching later this year.


2016 has barely begun and Joseph continues to cement his place among techno’s elite through  an immense presence at The BPM Festival in January, including a surprise guest DJ set at Dubfire’s Sci+Tec event, an epic back-to-back sunrise set with Jamie Jones during Paradise and a five hour extended set at his 100% JC party.


Joseph Capriati will make his way stateside with a pair of Music On dates at Marquee New York and Story in Miami before 100% JC at Trade kicks off Miami Music Week with Capriati playing all night. Earlier this month at Time Warp's flagship event in Germany his back-to-back set with Carl Cox was one for the books and they’ll be joining forces once again as Joseph will play the Carl Cox & Friends stage at Ultra Music Festival.
 

“The inspiration for 100% JC nights came from the festival and special guest DJ gigs that only allowed for shorter sets with a couple hours of DJing. For these concept nights, I can dedicate myself to longer extended sets and create original edits and remixes solely for this purpose. It’s a way for me to connect with my fans in a more meaningful way in intimate venues and really flex my DJing muscles." - Joseph Capriati
 

Clubbing bible Mixmag describes Joseph Capriati as “one of the best techno DJs on the planet”, which can be seen in the success of his party brand 100% JC where fans can enjoy Joseph’s trademark marathon sets around the world. Debuted in Barcelona during Sonar OFF Week 2014 and appearing at global hotspots such as The BPM Festival and Sonus Festival, 100% JC allows Joseph to truly demonstrate his innate magnetism and skills in the DJ booth.
