FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE


Saturday, November 7th, 2009
Pacha NYC presents
STEVE LAWLER
Extended Set
VIVa Music North American Tour 2009
Along with Jimmy Hill

Pacha NYC presents Steve Lawler as he hits the road for a 12-stop North American tour along with Jimmy Hill to bring forth his VIVa Music concept with a special extended set in NYC this Saturday, November 7th, 2009.

VIVa Music label boss Steve Lawler is fresh from being nominated again as ‘Best Ibiza DJ’ at the DJ Awards 2009 and with 12 releases in the last 12 months it’s been a lively year. This year saw prolific output coming in the form of original productions (Femme Fatale, Kalimba, Hocus Pocus etc) and remixes (Bat for Lashes “Daniel”, Radio Slave “Koma Koma’”). One of the label’s most successful releases was last year’s pioneering ‘On the Bus’ EP, which saw Steve become the first DJ to build a studio on a tour bus; the result was the 3 track EP release with his touring buddies at the time - Tiefschwarz and Audiofly. Now this EP is getting a complete reworking with three brand new remix EPs with re-rubs by Radio Slave, VIVA artists Syrossian & Nyra, Patrick Zigon, Hungarian artist Son-Tec, Tolga Fiden, French Maestro Mr. David K and another budding VIVA artist Leon.

Look out for new VIVa Music releases from Dualton & Masuki, Yvel & Tristan, Delete, and Horatio available on iTunes and Beatport.
