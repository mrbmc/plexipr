FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Insomniac and Marquee Dayclub at The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas Present
“WET WONDERLAND”

Weekly event launches Sunday, April 29, with America’s #1 DJ Kaskade for the 2012 Pool Season


Tweet it - As the 2012 pool season quickly approaches, Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub at The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas and Insomniac – the producers of the Electric Daisy Carnival, Las Vegas – are thrilled to announce an exciting new weekly event, “WET WONDERLAND.” This incredible daytime party launches April 29 with Kaskade and will take place every Sunday poolside at Marquee Dayclub, featuring world-class artists and performers, and cutting-edge production.

Insomniac’s history as a pioneering producer of the world’s most prominent and innovative electronic dance music events combined with Marquee’s history leading the electronic dance music charge in Las Vegas nightclubs and commitment to providing fans with the ultimate clubbing destination guarantees that Wet Wonderland will be a can’t-miss experience when it launches this April 29.

“Insomniac is an unrivaled event producer in the dance music world, and we could not think of a better collaborator to team up with to introduce what is sure to be an electrifying daytime party to close out each weekend,” said Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub partner Jason Strauss.

“The Dayclub experience is an incredible place to showcase what Marquee and Insomniac do best - together,” said Insomniac’s CEO, Pasquale Rotella. “Wet Wonderland will be a unique Dayclub event and we’re so excited to work with the Marquee family to make it happen.”

Marquee’s resident world-class DJ talent, along with special surprise guests will headline the weekly extravaganza at the venue, which will be enhanced for the 2012 season with increased staging design to provide for incredible production and performances.

Kaskade, America’s number 1 DJ as named by DJ Times, will headline the launch. His latest album Fire & Ice, scored nine Top 10 hits on Billboard’s Hot Dance Airplay Chart, and though an industry veteran, Kaskade was proclaimed the “new face of electronic dance music,” byThe New York Times.

Once open for the season on April 21, the Dayclub will be open daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. For more information or reservations please call702.333.9000.

Tickets are available at www.marqueelasvegas.com

About Marquee Dayclub
Marquee Dayclub at The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas brings the energy and excitement of Marquee Nightclub to the outdoors, embracing sunny days of the Las Vegas Strip with a lavish, adults-only offering. Spanning 22,000 square feet of the expansive multi-level 60,000 square-foot David Rockwell-designed entertainment complex, the Dayclub boasts two pools, several bars and a gaming area, along with exclusive cabanas with infinity edge pools and bungalows complete with overnight quarters and open-air party decks. Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub is from the world renowned nightlife impresarios behind such New York and Las Vegas destinations as TAO and LAVO.  The Las Vegas outpost of Marquee, a Manhattan nightlife fixture, debuted as Las Vegas welcomed 2011 and quickly established itself as a certified global dance music hot spot.

About Insomniac Events
Insomniac produces innovative concerts and music festivals enhanced by state-of-the-art lighting design, large-scale art installations, interactive circus and theatrical performances, and audience participation.  Fan safety and the quality of our fans’ experience are the company’s top priorities.  Insomniac’s premier annual event, Electric Daisy Carnival, is one of the biggest music festivals in the United States. Insomniac has produced more than 250 music events for more than 2,000,000 concertgoers in California, Nevada, Colorado, Texas, Florida, and Puerto Rico.  Founded by Pasquale Rotella, Insomniac has been based in Los Angeles since it started in 1993.
