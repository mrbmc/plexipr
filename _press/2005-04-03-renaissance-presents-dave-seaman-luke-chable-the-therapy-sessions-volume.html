RENAISSANCE Presents DAVE SEAMAN & LUKE CHABLE
The Therapy Sessions Volume 2
North American Tour Dates

RENAISSANCE & DAVE SEAMAN return with another installment of The Therapy Sessions. This year budding Australian DJ/producer LUKE CHABLE handles the guest disc honors with a tough, breaks-fuelled extravaganza that draws heavily on his fellow Melbourne compatriots, alongside some of his own acclaimed tracks and remixes.
