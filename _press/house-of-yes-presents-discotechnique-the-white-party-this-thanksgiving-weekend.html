House of Yes presents Discotechnique: The White Party 

Secret Guest Headliner plus Paul Raffaele, David Kiss, DJ Bruce & Blu Detiger

An All White Costume Disco Party Dedicated In Memory to NYC Clubbing Pioneer and Legend RIP David Mancuso

FREE BEFORE 11PM With Eventbrite RSVP
Tickets + RSVP Info
10pm-4am

Photo Credit: David Cova | House of Yes

House of Yes once again welcomes back the return of Discotechnique for a night of total disco fever FREE before 11pm with Eventbrite RSVP with their White Party edition on Saturday, November 26. Together with a stellar local lineup, the night will be dedicated in memory to NYC club pioneer and legend David Mancuso and will feature a special guest headliner that is often referred to as one of his proteges. Alongside an all New York lineup, the night will be filled with unique and funky beats synonymous with the Golden Era of nightlife. Accompanying the disco tunes will be the jaw-dropping aerial entertainment partygoers have come to know and love at House of Yes.Upon arrival guests will be bedazzled by pearly white decor, glistening mirror balls, soaring neon-illuminated trapeze swings, and hidden surprises derived from David Mancuso’s world famous The Loft parties. To guarantee entry, partygoers are expected to come dressed in all white attire in order to contribute to the opulent atmosphere that will ooze elegance while staying true to the 70s glamorous disco style that emphasizes clean lines and bright looks.

As one of House of Yes’ most popular monthly parties, previous installments of Discotechnique have featured themes like ‘All Gold ‘and ‘Filthy Rich’ and have been headlined by DJ legends such as Studio 54’s Nicky Siano and New York City nightclub stalwart Cosmo Baker, expect the perfect soundtrack from an influential figure that helped shaped New York City’s clubbing and dance music community. Joining the surprise guest will be NYC dance music staple Paul Raffaele, local Brooklyn favorite David Kiss, New York via Philadelphia native DJ Bruce, and multi-instrumentalist Blu Detiger.

About House of Yes:
House of Yes opened its third iteration in Bushwick at the beginning of 2016 to become a beacon of NYC nightlife after shuttering its doors too soon in East Williamsburg three years ago. Original partners Anya Sapozhnikova and Kae Burke, teamed up with Ilan Telmont and Justin Ahiyon to build out the best venue ever with a little help from their friends, seeding a Kickstarter campaign for $92,340 (original goal: $60,000). The space includes an art-deco restaurant parlor, a spectacularly decorated warehouse-sized performance space, a courtyard with kitschy repurposed decor, and a clandestine mini-club. House of Yes is now the home of circus spectacles, immersive cinema, burlesque and cabaret shows, aerial extravaganzas, nightlife parties, morning raves, brunch, BBQs, and bubble baths, all with superior sound and exemplary service. Each night is specially curated to create an immersive experience with music, theatrics, and performances to titillate the senses and expand your mind, unlike any other venue in Brooklyn right now.
