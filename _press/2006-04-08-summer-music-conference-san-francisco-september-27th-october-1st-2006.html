FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Summer Music Conference San Francisco
September 27th - October 1st, 2006

Announcing the SUMMER MUSIC CONFERENCE (SMC), the first ever digital music focused event on the West Coast, in San Francisco. This inaugural event will build on the momentum started by Loveparade San Francisco and continue to grow annually. The SMC mission is to bring together professionals in digital music, media, and technology, to generate new opportunities, create synergies and identify trends for the future.
