Made Event presents LAURENT GARNIER in his first NYC appearance in over 3 years at Cielo on Saturday, November 10th, 2007. This long-awaited appearance from one of the great DJ maestros will be an intimate dance music excursion with Garnier's eclectic programming and beautiful sound mosaic of a variety of dance-related genres. His last appearance at the closing parties for Centro-Fly (RIP 2004) is already the stuff of DJ legend.

PUBLIC OUTBURST, the latest release from top French DJ / producer and F Communications label boss Laurent Garnier, is an album of live sessions inspired by seventies improv jazz and recorded from sessions during the summer of 2006 featuring his live band. The seven tracks on the album were composed and performed by Garnier and his band consisting of Bugge Wesseltoft and Benjamin Rippert on keyboards, and Philippe Nadaud on brass. The album brings Laurent's unmistakable brand of techno into the live arena with the quartet jamming and improvising as jazz musicians with new tracks and jams as well as teasing us with a few classics from the vaults.

Laurent Garnier 'Public Outburst' out now on F Communications.

About Made Event
