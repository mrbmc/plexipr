About Made Event
The Made Event team, headed by Mike Bindra and Laura De Palma, is committed to excellence in the programming, promotion, and production of electronic dance music events. They continually bring a world-renowned caliber of entertainment into a variety of distinctive and innovative venues, and seek to push the limit of expertise in their field. With over 20 years of combined experience, Made Event has successfully produced inaugural international DJ events in Central Park and Brooklyn\'s Tobacco Warehouse and this August 17-18th, 2007, they present their award-winning Made in Central Park NYC event with Paul van Dyk for the 5th consecutive year in a row.

About Integral Sound
Integral Sound systems are renowned world-wide for their purity, precision and high performance. They provide custom solutions of proven system designs, incorporating custom built components, and acoustical engineering for each unique venue. Integral Sound principle and sound veteran Steve Dash has designed legendary sound systems at international clubs such as Cocoon (Germany), Home (London) and Twilo (NYC). This Winter Music Conference, with his partners Mike Bindra and George Stavro they will be specially trucking in equipment on an 18-wheeler tractor-trailer for parties at two Miami nightclubs: B.E.D. and Pawn Shop to bring you a truly immersive and powerful sound environment.
