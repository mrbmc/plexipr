AREA Event presents
Day Dream Winter Party
SEBASTIAN INGROSSO
[Swedish House Mafia]

The Pool After Dark at Harrah's Resort
Atlantic City, NJ
President's Day Weekend
Sunday, February 19

Listen to Sebastian Ingrosso's Refune Radio Podcast on iTunes

Dance music powerhouse Swedish House Mafia’s Sebastian Ingrosso and special guests heat up this President’s Day Weekend with the Day Dream Winter Party at The Pool After Dark at Harrah's Resort on Sunday, February 19 in Atlantic City, NJ. Also known as one-third of the infamous Swedish House Mafia, Ingrosso is responsible for the ultimate summer anthem “Calling” with Alesso, showcasing his talent for understanding the world of melodies just as much as the power of the kick drum -- and for sending the dancefloor into a frenzy.

Having released a steady stream of original tracks and remixes for over a decade, Ingrosso produced the modern day vocal house classic “How Soon Is Now” with Dirty South, David Guetta and Julia McKnight, the searing remix of “Jack You” with Steve Angello for Diddy and Felix, which was approved by Sean Combs himself, as well as remixes of MGMT’s “Kids” and Miike Snow’s “Silvia.” 2010 saw him co-write and co-produce ‘Cupid Boy’, one of the key electronic anthems on Kylie’s new Number 1 album. As head of A & R for his label, Refune, Sebastian is constantly discovering and developing new talents such as Style of Eye, AN21, Max Vangeli, True Identity and his most recent signing, the exceptional Lune. Meanwhile, Ingrosso’s achievements as a DJ are no less impressive, as he regularly performs for crowds in the thousands all over the globe.

Sebastian Ingrosso presents his new free downloadable 60 minute monthly Refune Radio show for your aural pleasure! Featuring all the biggest tracks from Seb’s SD card, exclusive music you won’t hear anywhere else, live reports from his sell out shows and Seb dishing the dirt and backstage gossip.
