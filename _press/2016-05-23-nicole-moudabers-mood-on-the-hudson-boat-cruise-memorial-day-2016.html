Nicole Moudaber’s 'MOOD on the Hudson' Boat Cruise
Memorial Day
Monday, May 30, 2016
In the MOOD Radio Episode 108 - Live from MoodRAW, Beirut
Nicole Moudaber will return to the Big Apple with an intimate boat cruise around picturesque Manhattan on Memorial Day, Monday, May 30 to kick off summer. The MOOD Records label head will take revelers on a journey from sunset into the glittering night playing an open to close set with NYC’s iconic skyline as a backdrop sure to be the perfect way to end the holiday weekend. Nicole continues to achieve new milestones as she recently hosted her first, highly acclaimed festival stage takeover, Nicole Moudaber Presents MoodZONE, at the New York edition of Electric Daisy Carnival.
While fans in New York are dancing out at sea, Monday, May 30 will also see the release of Nicole’s remix for Coyu & Ramiro Lopez’s “That’s Not Happening” on Bedrock Records; a track fans may have heard in sets since Ultra Music Festival. Nicole has a stretch of North American tour dates with stops at Chicago’s Spring Awakening Festival and Electric Daisy Carnival Las Vegas in addition to a special MoodDAY Miami event before she returns to Europe for the summer season including Paradise’s Ibiza opener at DC10 and festivals throughout Europe.
In addition to her performances, Nicole will be speaking on panels at the International Music Summit (IMS) in Ibiza, “Striving for Excellence & The Art of Playing Differently” on May 26 and “Diversity in Electronic Music: A Vote For Vertical Proliferation in association with Shesaidso” on May 27 as well as at EDMBiz’s “Beyond the Boys’ Club: What’s Next For Women in Dance Music” during EDC Week in Las Vegas.
MOOD on the Hudson tickets are available while supplies last on Resident Advisor and Wantickets.
