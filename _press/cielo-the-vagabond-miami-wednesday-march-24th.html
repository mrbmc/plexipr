New York City’s renowned nightclub Cielo brings a lineup of its residents and favorites to downtown Miami for Conference 2010 at The Vagabond on Wednesday, March 24th, 2010.

Multiple award winning venue and New York clubbing institution Cielo returns to the sunny shores of Miami, and brings an impressive lineup of world class headliners, as well as its favorite resident DJ’s.  Known throughout the clubbing world as one of the finest clubs for top notch electronic dance music, the Cielo brand has become synonymous with quality music, design, and sound.  This year the prestigious Club World Awards have, once again, nominated Cielo in multiple categories including Best Club, Best Party (Dance.Here.Now.), and Best Resident DJ (Francois K, for his long-running Deep Space Monday nights).  And on the global stage, the International Dance Music Awards have nominated Cielo for Best U.S. Club and Best Soundsystem (Funktion One).

Cielo will take over downtown Miami hot spot The Vagabond for one night with three separate areas of music to display distinctive musical styles throughout the night to whet every appetite. The main room’s deep house, tech-house grooves will move the body. The side room will showcase nu-disco, leftfield dance tunes and finally, the outdoor garden will host a mix of chill out and funky grooves. The eclectic line up begins with international sensation and Innervisions recording artist Alex from Tokyo.  House music maestro Behrouz is as much a favorite on a New York dancefloor as he is in his adopted hometown of Miami.  Adult Section’s Darshan Jesrani and Dennis Kane bring their eclectic disco grooves from their popular Cielo residency.  Ibiza mainstay DJ Pippi has been mixing records since the seventies and has held some of the most prestigious residencies on the white isle.  Jojo Flores is a global ambassador of soulful house and eclectic DJ sets from his Therapy nights and his own GotSoul Records imprint. Cadenza favorite Michel Cleis’ productions are consistently heard on dancefloors around the world including last year’s WMC anthem “La Mezcla.”  Nickodemus has provided New York with his legendary Tuntables on the Hudson events for over a decade now.  Cielo resident & co-owner Nicolas Matar combines elements of deep house and tech-house with soulful and disco influences regularly on his Paradizo residency nights.  Circoloco favorite Tania Vulcano’s style of deep, percussive lead house music has made her a favorite of the world’s top venues.  Tedd Patterson is well revered amongst house music circles as one of the greats.  His Vibal nights bring a virtual who’s who of house legends to the legendary Cielo booth.  Cielo brings a special live performance from Spaniards Wagon Cookin with their fusion of jazz, soul, latin, brazil, funk and house.  Finally, Pacha Ibiza & Cielo resident Willie Graff brings his deep, soulful grooves and percussive Afro–Latin rhythms to round out the musical arsenal on this special night, when Cielo takes over Miami!

ABOUT CIELO
Located at 18 Little West 12th Street, in the heart of New York City’s trendy Meat Packing District, is open nightly from 10:00pm-4:00am. Cielo is a beautifully designed 350-capacity club featuring a world-class, state-of-the-art digital Funktion One soundsystem. Since its opening in 2003, Cielo has become an internationally renowned dance music nightclub brand and has upheld its edict of world-class music, professional service and all-inclusive atmosphere.

Over the last 7 years, Cielo has continued to triumph and collect accolades including; Club Awards 2009 winner for Best Party – Dance.Here.Now.; Club World Awards 2008 winner for Best Club (Winner 2005 & 2006) and Best Party – Dance.Here.Now.; Top 50 Nightclubs in the World- DJ Magazine 2006, 2007 & 2008; Best Nightclub- Village Voice 2006; Best Deep House Club- New York Magazine 2006; Best Resident DJ Francois K- Club World Awards 2006 & 2005; Best Venue USA- URB Magazine 2005; Best Interior Design- Club World Awards 2004; Best Club- Dancestar Awards USA 2004. For more info, please go to www.cieloclub.com.
