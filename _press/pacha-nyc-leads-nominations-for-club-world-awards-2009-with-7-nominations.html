FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE


Pacha NYC Leads Nominations For
Club World Awards 2009 With 7 Nominations

Best Superclub: Pacha
Best Resident DJ: Victor Calderone
Best LJ: Timmy Lights
Best VJ: United Vision
Best DJ Set: Danny Tenaglia, "Futurism" Album Release Party, August 2
Best DJ Set: David Guetta, Pacha NYC presents David Guetta, March 21
Best One-Off: Made Event & Pacha NYC present One Night Only, Miami

Pacha NYC rises to the top among 300 nominees in 24 categories for the Club World Awards 2009. After winning the coveted title of “Best Superclub” for 2 years straight since its opening in 2005, Pacha is holding it down as New York's most successful superclub. From the Club World Awards nominations issue, the excerpt on the club states, “With 30,00 square feet spread out over four floors and an admirable commitment to dance music and DJ culture, [Pacha] has helped set the superclub standard.”  Pacha NYC also received a nomination for "Best U.S. Club" from the International Dance Music Awards and will be included in this year's prestigious DJ Mag's annual round-up of the "Top 100 Clubs of the World".

On these proud achievements and award prospects, Pacha NYC president Eddie Dean says, “We are pleased just to be nominated for so many awards and it’s a credit to our Pacha team who work really hard all year-round to deliver the best clubbing experience each night. We’re looking forward to seeing if we can achieve ‘Best Superclub’ in North America for a record-breaking 3 years in a row.”

The Club World Award winners will be determined by a twelve person, industry-expert judges panel to be announced at the Winter Music Conference in Miami. Last year, Pacha NYC received a total of three of the coveted Club World Award 2008 Disco Globes including, ‘Best Resident LJ’ and ‘Best DJ Set’ by Danny Tenaglia, beating out competition from Hollywood, Las Vegas, Miami, and Toronto.

Pacha NYC will be making its annual pilgrimage to Miami for the Winter Music Conference, to bring the music and quality, cosmopolitan club goers everywhere have come to expect over the years. Pacha NYC : Miami 2009 events will take place all week long at top Miami venues: Parkwest, Shelborne, Surfcomber, and Dream.  For the entire week, Pacha NYC will also be taking over the Royal Palms Resort Hotel (1545 Collins Avenue, Miami Beach) as its de factor Miami HQ and for daytime pool parties.

About Pacha NYC
Pacha New York is the first North American branch of the international nightlife consortium. Pacha, the international brand was founded by Ricardo Urgell in 1967 with a single club in Sitges, Spain and the family-run business has since evolved into a global lifestyle brand that encompasses more than 25 nightclubs, a hotel, restaurants, a record label, radio station, lifestyle magazine and a full-scale merchandising division. Since opening in 2005, Pacha NYC has risen to the top of North America’s clubbing capital and has become a staple in NYC's nightlife, by attracting the most sought after global DJs with an international, cosmopolitan clubbing clientele. Week after week Pacha NYC delivers the very best clubbing nights with the venue's over-the-top debauchery, performances, spectacular sound, quality production and dazzling lighting. Pacha NYC has won numerous awards and distinctions including: Club World Awards 2008 awards for – Best Superclub, Best Resident LJ and Best DJ Set: Danny Tenaglia; Club World Awards 2007 – Best Superclub, Best New Venue, Best Soundsystem; DJ Mag Top US Club of the World 2008; AOL City’s Best 2008 & 2007 – Best Dance Club; Best Of Citysearch: New York Nightlife 2007 #1 Best Dance Club; and ‘Top 10 Hottest Megaclub in America’ by Maxim magazine.
