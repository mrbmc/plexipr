FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

 

MARQUEE NIGHTCLUB & DAYCLUB
AT THE COSMOPOLITAN OF LAS VEGAS

Stafford Brothers Return to Vegas
At Wet Wonderland on Sunday, August 26th

 

Australia’s #1 DJs (ITM Awards) the Stafford Brothers continue their global takeover this summer, returning to their residencies at Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub in Las Vegas and Marquee Sydney. Stafford Brothers lit the dancefloor on fire during their debut last month -- a performance that lived up to their “fun, fun, fun” reputation, according to Las Vegas Weekly.  “Marquee in Las Vegas was an amazing experience, the club is phenomenal and the production second to none. Marquee Sydney is definitely the hottest venue in Sydney now and we are proud to be part of it,” Matt Stafford says. "We are really looking forward to playing the Marquee pool, we went on the Saturday after our last Vegas show and it was rocking!" Stafford Brothers perform at Marquee Dayclub in Las Vegas for Wet Wonderland this Sunday, August 26th, alongside residents Gabriel & Dresden.

‘Take Control’ - the Stafford Brothers’ most recent release hit download stores last week via Roger Sanchez’s iconic house label Stealth Records. It’s the second release in as many months to take a Top 10 placing in the Beatport house charts riding the success of the previous title ‘Pressure’. Hot off the press, it hasn’t taken long for some of the biggest hit makers in the country to notice their talent with forthcoming remixes slated for Wolfgang Gartner, Krewella, Far East Movement, Timbaland and Neyo.

Not only are these brothers in arms on fire in the studio; Matt and Chris Stafford are a hit fixture across network TV in their home country down under. ‘The Stafford Brothers’ lend the title to their very own documentary series which over the last two seasons has followed the trials and tribulations of Australia’s most celebrated DJs and is syndicated in over 20 countries worldwide. Now based in LA, the third season is in the midst of production and slated for international release next year highlighting groundbreaking times for two DJ siblings destined for big things.

Get ready as the Stafford Brothers #TakeControl of Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub in Las Vegas and Marquee Sydney. Check here for upcoming dates.
