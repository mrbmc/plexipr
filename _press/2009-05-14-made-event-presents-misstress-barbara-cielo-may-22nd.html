FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Friday, May 22nd, 2009
Made Event presents
MISSTRESS BARBARA
@ Cielo

Made Event transports the Canadian star Mistress Barbara as she graces us with her light on Friday, May 22nd at Cielo.

Not afraid to expose her vulnerable side, Mistress Barbara speaks out her heart and soul as she embarks on a whole new adventure with the release of I’m No Human, her first album as a songwriter out on Maple Music Recordings in May 2009. One of the most remarkable aspects is that the album sees Barbara herself on vocals. Lush melodies overlay her minimalist beats accompanied her intoxicating femme fatale voice unraveling a happy development born out of unhappy circumstance.

The album is her most personal to date and inspired from a personal loss in the family, the passing of her late father.  “I know he would be very, very proud.  He played guitar, violin, piano,” Barbara says. On recording violins in the studio, she adds, “I had tears in my eyes. He would be so happy.

Pouring out her feelings in I’m No Human, Barbara shines on through a cover from Leonardo Cohen’s “Dance Me To The End of Love” on the album;  “Everything in my life is for love. I do everything for love and I undo everything for love.” Additional contributions from Sam Roberts, Brazilian Girls and Bjorn Yttling of Peter Bjorn and John.
