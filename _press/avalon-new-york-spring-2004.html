AVALON New York - SPRING 2004

Since its grand opening in September 2003, Avalon NY has ushered in a new era for NYC clubbing. By bringing in the biggest and best talent in underground electronic music, Avalon is fast-becoming a global clubbing destination. In recent months, we\'ve brought you Richie Hawtin, Josh Wink, Doc Martin, Mark Farina, Fery Corsten, Felix Da Housecat, Mauro Picotto, Donald Glaude, Charles Feelgood, Tall Paul, James Zabiela, Lee Burridge, Marcus Wyatt, Timo Maas, Nick warren, Dave Seaman, Steve Lawler, Sandra Collins and more.
