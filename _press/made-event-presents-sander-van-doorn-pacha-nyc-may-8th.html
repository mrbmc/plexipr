FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Friday, May 8th, 2009
Made Event presents
SANDER VAN DOORN
(Doorn Records)

Made Event presents the Dutch sensation, Sander Van Doorn at Pacha NYC on Friday, May 8th, 2009.

In this day and age, being a Trance DJ from the Netherlands is not a unique thing, however, the level of talent and amount of chart topping records and sold out shows is unique to Sander Van Doorn. Entering the DJ Mag Top 100 at 32, then climbing to 15 within a year is no small feat, and is exciting to see what lies ahead for this young talent.  These days he’s embracing the future and sharing his knowledge capital with his “Sander Studio Sessions”. The 3rd video installment is out now featuring Sander showcasing techniques and tips.

From the studio, Sander’s joint production with mentor and friend Marco V, “What Say” has been slaying dancefloors worldwide and will get a proper full release in May. Also just released on Doorn Records is killer track ‘Ummet Ozcan - Timewave Zero’, now topping off at #1 spot in the Beatport trance charts. Sander also recently mixed “Spaceman” from The Killers recent massive album, Day & Age. To further propagate the Sander van Doorn sound, Sander’s Identity radio show, now in its 3rd year on DI.FM showcases his newest productions, remixes, and hot crate of records to his adoring fans worldwide.
