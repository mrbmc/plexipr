FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

MARQUEE NIGHTCLUB & DAYCLUB
AT THE COSMOPOLITAN OF LAS VEGAS
June 2011


Kaskade at Marquee Nightclub on June 11


Friday, June 3
MISS NINE @ Dayclub

Miss Nine has taken the dance world by storm with her eclectic musical tastes, ranging from energetic grooves with plenty of vocals to a deeper progressive sound. Also a full-time model, she earned a residency in Amsterdam at the locally legendary Motion parties after only months on the decks, where she played alongside masters such as John Digweed, Dave Seaman, Nick Warren, and James Zabiela. Her ‘Yoshitoshi Ibiza’ mix for the world-renowned progressive and big room house label reached #1 on the iTunes Dance Album Chart within four months, followed by an album released on the world famous Armada label: 'Dancefloor Sessions'. Miss Nine also hosts her own radio show, Nine Sessions, on more than 30 radio stations worldwide, including DI.fm, Proton Radio and Frisky Radio.

Friday, June 3
MAX VANGELI @ Nightclub

"One of the most promising artists for 2011" as described by MTV.com, Max Vangeli has grabbed the attention of electronic music fans worldwide. Last year, his massive remix of Pendulum's “The Island” with Steve Angello and AN21 was named “hottest track in the world” by BBC Radio One's Zane Lowe and “Swedish Beauty” was named Essential New Tune by Pete Tong. Max Vangeli is due to release his upcoming debut album ‘People of the Night,’ which he describes as “dark techy beats to big melodic house,” with AN21 on Steve Angello’s Size Records later this year. Meanwhile, his schedule is packed full of international dates at the world’s best clubs and festivals.

Saturday, June 4
DAVID MORALES @ Dayclub

A true vanguard of dance music, David Morales’ achievements span over 25 years. Nominated for multiple Grammy Awards, he won Remixer of the Year in 1998. He is responsible for dancefloor hits like “In de Ghetto,” “Needin’ U,” “How Would U Feel” and many more, and has remixed and produced over 500 releases for artists including Mariah Carey, Britney Spears, Michael Jackson, Janet Jackson, Eric Clapton, Pet Shop Boys, U2, Whitney Houston, and Jamiroquai to name a few. He co-owns and operates Def Mix Productions representing Frankie Knuckles, Satoshi Tomiie and Hector Romero, while continuing to tour the world and working on a new studio album due out this year.

Saturday, June 4
SANDER VAN DOORN ‘DUSK TILL DOORN’ + DAVID MORALES @ Nightclub

Marquee resident Sander Van Doorn returns to wow the crowd with his Dusk Till Doorn show. Named after his compilation album of the same name, the production’s spectacular sound and visuals are fully integrated with his addicting fusion of trance, electro, techno and house. Sander’s accolades include the #12 spot in DJ Mag’s Top 100 DJs poll, remixes for the likes of Robbie Williams, The Killers and Depeche Mode, and headlining appearances at events like Creamfields, Mysteryland and Electric Zoo Festival. Besides his busy production schedule, he brings his immensely popular ‘Identity’ radio show on Sirius XM to fans every week.

He is joined at Marquee Nightclub by David Morales.

Monday, June 6
ERIC D-LUX @ Nightclub

Hailing from Los Angeles, Power 106 mixer Eric D-Lux first gained national recognition with his “Buko Breaks Vol 1-3” mix tape. With his raw, up-beat, and straight from the streets personality, this artist has regularly been bringing dancefloor destroyers to Marquee Las Vegas while continuing to drop mixtapes and running the underground scene.

Friday, June 10
BLAKE JARRELL @ Dayclub

Blake Jarrell is one of the fastest rising stars in the dance music industry today. Beside flying across the globe and back playing such locations as Ibiza, Dubai, Singapore and Los Angeles, he’s also continuously putting out new original music and remix work. With a mix compilation for Armada Dancefloor Sessions series out, his hit “Punta del Este” was featured on Armin’s acclaimed ‘A State of Trance 2008’ compilation and his remix of Moussa Clark’s “She Wants Him” stayed in the #1 position on Beatport’s trance charts for several weeks. Blake’s monthly radio show, ‘Concentrate,’ features signature mix sets and special international guests.

Friday, June 10
AXWELL @ Nightclub

Axwell is a superstar DJ one of the most successful and influential remixersof the 21st century, as evidenced by the Grammy nomination for his remix with Dirty South of Temper Trap’s “Sweet Disposition.” Axwell’s Midas-like touch has made him the dance hit go-to guy for artists like Madonna, Nelly Furtado, Moby, Flo Rida and Pharrell Williams. Not to mention his original tracks like “Nothing But Love,” a masterful house number featuring fantastic vocals by Errol Reid. On top of what is an already successful solo career, he is one third of Swedish House Mafia with Sebastian Ingrosso and Steve Angello, responsible for smash hits like “One,” “Miami 2 Ibiza” and “Leave The World Behind.” This year, Axwell’s busy touring schedule included the world’s top festivals and clubs, helping to lead him to the Top 10 spot in the DJ Mag Top 100 DJ Poll.

Saturday, June 11
KASKADE @ Nightclub

Kaskade’s Marquee Las Vegas residency, recently attended by the likes of Reggie Bush, the rapper Nas and more, invites clubbers into a spectacular fantasy world, full of beautiful ballerina dancers and thrilling trance, house and pop music, as he indulges his every brilliant artistic impulse and keeps everyone packed on the dancefloor. Kaskade’s soul-lifting electronic music and unmatched versatility have recently earned him International Dance Music Award nominations for ‘Best American DJ (North America),’ ‘Best Full Length DJ Mix,’ ‘Best Artist (Solo)’ and ‘Best Progressive Track’ for “Fire in Your New Shoes” feat. Dragonette. With an IDMA ‘Best Remixer’ Award already under his belt as well as a spot in DJ Mag’s Top 100 DJs, Kaskade also has several number one iTunes singles and earned headline billing at major music festivals around the world. Last year he released his sixth artist album Dynasty, which debuted at number 4 on Billboard’s Dance/Electronic Albums chart, as well as the ‘dance.love’ mix compilation.

Monday, June 13
ERIC D-LUX + EC TWINS @ Nightclub

Named after a successful club they once created in the U.K. (Eye Candy), the EC Twin’s European dance-influenced house music and energetic performances have earned them passionate fan bases from Northern Europe to South East Asia. Hailing from Manchester, the handsome and talented duo have performed at the hottest nightclubs and festivals around the world. Their long list of production work includes their hit remix of Andy Caldwell’s “Scream,” “Doing it Well” with Tim B of Second Sun and Paul Harris, and more on Oakenfold’s Perfecto label. Known for their wildly kinetic sessions at Guys and Dolls in Los Angeles, they regularly electrify the dancefloor at Marquee.

Friday, June 17
VIKTORIA METZKER @ Dayclub
EC TWINS with VIKTORIA METZKER @ Nightclub

Hailing from Budapest, Viktoria Metzker is a successful DJ and model whose picture has adorned Playboy magazine covers in 10 countries. Since starting her DJ career in 2007, she has been spinning all around the world as well as developing her electro house-vocal style of singing, which she incorporates in performances. Every other month, she DJs the hugely popular Hungarian radio show 'Radio 1 Ministry of Sound. Viktoria Metzker has worked with Dutch superstar Funkerman and the German DJ and producer Eddie Thoneick and she recently released the mix compilation ““Ibiza World Club Tour Vol. 2.”

EC Twins headline the Nightclub with Viktoria Metzker joining.

Saturday, June 18
SERGE DEVANT @ Dayclub

After consistently wowing fans with his hallmark high-octane and uplifting sets during his residency at NYC’s world-renown Crobar in 2006, Serge turned his sights on production beginning with his anthemic take on Beloved’s “Sweet Harmony” the following year. The track garnered acclaim from the likes of Pete Tong. Soon after, Serge Devant’s name appeared on his own exclusive Coca-Cola bottle as a part of the company’s Beijing Olympics campaign. His first album in 2009, ‘Wanderer,’ is a cinematic piece lead by “Addicted,” an instant classic that hit #1 across Europe. Currently working on his second album, set to be released in the fall of 2011, Serge Devant continues to tour bringing his hard-hitting, riveting and classically beautiful sound to fans worldwide. Meanwhile his recently released remix of “3 AM Eternal” has gained the support of Sander Kleinenberg, Erik Morillo , EDX, and more.

Saturday, June 18
COSMIC GATE with SERGE DEVANT @ Nightclub

For over 11 boundary-pushing years, Nic Chagall and Bossi have developed Cosmic Gate into one of the most creative and highly-sought-after electronic music acts on the planet. Four hugely-received albums have transported tracks like “Not Enough Time,” “Body of Conflict,” “Analog Feel,” “F.A.V.” and classics including “Exploration of Space” and “Fire Wire” onto a now uncountable number of dancefloors. As DJs, the effortless cool of their kinetic, enthralling live sets are seen by hundreds of thousands of club and festival goers annually. It's this perfect storm of production and spinning power that saw them become the highest-climbers in DJ Mag's 2009 Top 100 chart and land at no. 24 in 2010. Regulars at Marquee, they are joined this month by Emma Hewitt, whose beautiful vocals are featured on “Not Enough Time.” Serge Devant also joins them for a live performance at the Nightclub.

Monday, June 20
VICE + REDFOO ‘Party Rock Mondays’ @ Nightclub

DJ Vice’s illustrious career started out at Los Angeles radio station Power 106, which gained him a fan in none other than the legend Dr. Dre. Since then, he’s spun at the biggest parties around the world, lent creative input to artists like Timbaland, Kanye and Pharrell, and opened two locations of his own sneaker store, CRSVR, one of which is located right inside The Cosmopolitan Hotel of Las Vegas, alongside the Marquee nightclub itself. DJ Vice is known for crossing genres with ease: not just hip hop, but house, dance and rock music too. Now a Las Vegas nightlife fixture, he spins regularly to a packed house at Marquee.

Redfoo of LMFAO and his crew have been bringing madness to Vegas for his Party Rock Mondays residency at Marquee every month, with live vocal performances, customized videos, blow up zebras, go-go dancers decked out in party rock gear, and the perfect electro house party rock mix. This wildly energetic and technically flawless DJ received a 2010 Grammy nomination for LMFAO’s debut album, ‘Party Rock,’ and has collaborated with some of the industry’s best, including David Guetta, the Black Eyed Peas, Lil Jon and Pitbull. Not to mention his hit remixes for Kanye West’s “Love Lockdown” and Katy Perry’s “Hot N Cold.” LMFAO recently released the single “Party Rock Anthem,” from their upcoming second album ‘Sorry For Party Rocking.’

Friday, June 24
CHUCKIE ‘DIRTY DUTCH RED LIGHT DISTRICT’ @ Nightclub

With his Diddy Dirty Money “I Hate That You Love Me” Chuckie Marquee remix taking the world by storm, Chuckie continues to adopt Amsterdam’s risque XXX logo and bring the city’s daring, sophisticated and sexy style to Marquee’s dancefloor every month for his Dirty Dutch Red Light District residency. Chuckie is the mastermind behind the Dirty Dutch phenomenon, famous for its addictive “house with an attitude influenced by hip hop” sound and global house music events. Chuckie’s monster tracks like “Let The Bass Kick” have been supported by the likes of Swedish House Mafia, Laidback Luke and Pete Tong, and he has remixed David Guetta and Bob Sinclar, among others. His busy touring schedule also includes playing headlining sets at some of the world’s biggest festivals as well as a monthly rotation on the legendary BBC Radio 1’s ‘In New DJs We Trust’ show. He recently released ‘Chuckie Presents: Dirty Dutch Digital Vol. 04,’ featuring 19 exclusive tracks.

Saturday, June 25
ROGER SANCHEZ + DIRTY SOUTH @ Dayclub

Marquee resident and Grammy award-winning DJ Roger Sanchez earned his status as the go-to DJ for pop/house hits with recent remixes of Maroon 5, OneRepublic and Brandon Flowers, and previous remixes for Daft Punk, Diana Ross and No Doubt (which won him a Grammy). His contributions to the Blade soundtrack, early hits like “Luv Dancin”, “Another Chance” and “Release Yo Self” and world tours earned the New York native a global audience. This month he releases a brand new single “Worldwide,” followed by the ‘Release Yourself 11’ compilation, a legendary house music mix series that has grown into an International Dance Music Award-winning podcast and global clubbing concept. For his Release Yourself residency at Marquee, Roger transforms the club into a sexy burlesque boudoir.

Joining Roger will be fellow Marquee resident Dirty South, who earlier this year was nominated for the ‘Best Remixed Recording’ Grammy award along with Swedish House Mafia’s Axwell for their remix of The Temper Trap’s “Sweet Disposition.” He has also worked with dance music giants David Guetta and Sebastian Ingrosso (“How Soon Is Now”) and remixed some of music’s biggest names, including U2, Snoop Dogg and Depeche Mode. In between his heavy touring and producing schedule, Dirty South regularly releases compilations for labels such as Ministry Of Sound, Toolroom Records and SONY just to name a few. Last year saw the birth of his hit track “Phazing” from his label of the same name, which properly launched as a global clubbing concept, Starphazing, at Marquee Las Vegas and showcases his signature dark house sound and hands-in-the-air good vibes. With a hit remix of “Coming Home” by Diddy Dirty Money (feat. Skylar Grey) out now, this month Dirty South releases ‘Strictly Dirty South,’ a 2-CD set featuring legendary label Strictly Rhythm classics and Dirty South originals and remixes.

Saturday, June 25
ROGER SANCHEZ ‘RELEASE YOURSELF’ with Tatiana Fontes @ Nightclub

Sanchez will also be performing later that evening at Marquee Nightclub along with Tatiana Fontes, who brings fusion of the ‘80s and rock from her youth (Duran Duran, The Cure, and the like), along with European house and electronic music, drawn from her years working as an international model. The Brazilian-born DJ grew up in the music business, with a father who owned a nightclub and brothers who were professional DJs. Now residing in Los Angeles, Tatiana DJs regularly at the hottest clubs and parties around the world and works alongside the industry’s best, including Erick Morillo, Sharam, Bob Sinclair, Dirty South, Armand Van Helden, Cedric Gervais and Markus Schulz.

Sunday, June 26
AVICII + GLENN MORRISON @ Dayclub

Avicii returns again to Marquee to destroy the dancefloor with his unique house sound. Earlier this year, he won the International Dance Music Award for Best Breakthrough Solo Artist, which comes as no surprise, as Tiesto already named him Breakthrough DJ/Producer of 2010 in DJ Mag. Avicii has stamped his sound on remixes for Bob Sinclar, Dizzee Rascal, Sophie Ellis Baxter, Little Boots and David Guetta, and has collaborated with Tiesto, Sebastian Ingrosso from Swedish House Mafia and many more. His track “Seek Bromance” is a gold-selling radio sensation, while his remix of Armin van Buuren’s “Drowning” is currently one of the most sought-after house tracks in the world. He is joined by Glenn Morrisson, a DJ, remixer, label owner who’s deep passion for music can be felt permeating throughout his signature style of melodic trance and energetic house -- and his reputation for demolishing dancefloors. He has released a slew of notable dance records through his acclaimed label Morrison Recordings, including his EPs ‘Odyssey,’ ‘Graffiti/You Plus Me Equals This’ and Drone,’ which have all received extensive plays from DJ heavyweights like John Digweed, Sasha, and Sander Kleinenberg. His next album, titled ‘Mine & Yours’ is out soon.
