Pacha NYC proudly presents rising Dutch superstar Chuckie as he takes over the Pacha NYC Dance Parade float and then makes his debut later in the evening at Pacha NYC on Saturday, May 22nd, 2010.

March with Chuckie & Pacha NYC at the annual New York City Dance Parade! Chuckie will be at the helm of Pacha NYC’s Dance Parade float--leading a parade of dancers to celebrate electronic dance music - Dirty Dutch style! The parade kicks off at 1PM at 20th and 21st between Park and Fifth Avenue and heads down Broadway with the house, techno, and disco floats taking you into the heart of the East Village.  The annual event culminates with an eclectic DanceFest in Tompkins Square Park. Find out all the information about New York’s annual Dance Parade, celebrating all types of dance, at www.danceparade.org.

Join the Dance Parade:  www.pachanyc.com/danceparade
Where: 5th Avenue between 20th 21st Streets NYC
When: Saturday, May 22nd at 1pm (arrive at 12:30pm)

Chuckie has been making a name for himself around the world by putting his signature deep, dirty house sound on remixes for David Guetta, Bob Sinclar and Joachim Gaurraud. His talent for building insanely intense climaxes, then delivering devastatingly explosive beats, is no child’s play. Killer original tracks like “Let The Bass Kick” have launched him into the top 10 British Singles Charts and won him praise from Swedish House Mafia, Laidback Luke and Pete Tong. In summer 2008, David Guetta handpicked Chuckie to play at his event at Pacha in Ibiza, saying “he is mixing the hip-hop vibe with house music like no one else!” Chuckie has won numerous accolades, including the Dutch TMF “Best Dance DJ” Award last year, for which he beat out super DJs Tiësto and Armin Van Buuren. This year, he also won the inaugural Best Remix Data Award in L.A. for his remix of David Guetta’s “Sexy Bitch.”

In 2005, Chuckie created the now-legendary, always sold-out “Dirty Dutch” house music event, attended by over thirty thousand music lovers. Dirty Dutch made its WMC debut at Cameo in Miami this year, featuring Chuckie himself, Estelle, Afrojack and Sidney Samson (among others), and he plans to bring the party to selected cities around the globe. Indeed, Chuckie is taking over the U.S. in 2010 with a freshly inked multi-album deal with Atlantic Records and work with stars like Jermaine Dupri, P. Diddy, Lil Jon, Toni Braxton and Akon.

Check out Chuckie’s website for music, videos, photo galleries, interviews, upcoming events, his weekly Dirty Dutch Radio program and more at www.djchuckie.com.
