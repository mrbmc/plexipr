Made Event hosts showcases for two of the most accomplished & unswerving independent dance labels in their respective genres-- Anjunabeats and MINUS, both of which are celebrating dance music milestones. Over the last 8 years, trance music’s true artisans Above & Beyond have cultivated Anjunabeats from its backroom beginnings to the apex of worldwide dance recognition. Now their brainchild enters the threshold of its 100th release with a series of album release parties around the world. Richie Hawtin’s esteemed techno label MINUS will be celebrating their landmark Ten Year Anniversary 1998 | 2008. The tenth anniversary year will be marked by MINUS showcases at Fabric, WMC, DEMF, and Sonar with special MINUS events hosted throughout the year and throughout the world, along with a year of new releases, new Artists and new localized technology.*All guestlist requests must be submitted by March 10th. Anjunabeats celebrates its landmark 100th release with a new album ‘Above & Beyond Anjunabeats 100’ and parties around the world. This date will be the first full blown Anjunabeats night on US soil. Joining Above & Beyond in the Anjunabeats Room will be Anjuna legends Super8 & Tab, Air For Life collaborator Andy Moor and Sweden's hottest trance talent Mike Shiver. Meanwhile the Anjunadeep room sees Anjuna debuts for Fine Taste (Johan Vermeulen & Freek Geuze - aka Chunk & Twist, Interplay) and Boom Jinx, ably supported by Electrobios and label A&R James Grant.

About Made Event
The Made Event team, headed by Mike Bindra and Laura De Palma, is committed to excellence in the programming, promotion, and production of electronic dance music events. They continually bring a world-renowned caliber of entertainment into a variety of distinctive and innovative venues, and seek to push the limit of expertise in their field. With over 20 years of combined experience, and over 300 successful events produced in New York City and at Winter Music Conference, Made Event is an industry leader.
