ƱZ To Release ‘Magellan’ EP
Debut Release on Quality Goods Records Out May 20
 
[Soundcloud] ƱZ - "Magellan" [please post!]

“Masked and mysterious producer ƱZ is considered one of the pillars of the trap genre, and has just further solidified that distinction with the creation of his new label, Quality Goods Records.” — Beatport
 

 

Trap enigma ƱZ releases the debut track, “Magellan,” from his brand new Quality Goods Records label launched last month (read more via Billboard). Like the explorer of the same name, "Magellan" sees ƱZ’s stuttering snares and hi-hats circumnavigate hypnotizing kick drums and gleaming synths for a dark and addicting track fit for the bedroom or the club. Listen here.

Following the completion of his influential ‘Trap Shit’ series and Frontier EP released last year, the ‘Magellan’ EP sees ƱZ pushing into fresh musical territories to kick off the new era of Quality Goods Records. The 'Magellan' EP is out May 20 and will contain collaborations with ATLiens, BeazyTymes, and a feature from Los Angeles hip hop group The Rej3ctz.

Pre-order ‘Magellan’ on iTunes at hyperurl.co/UZMagellanEP.

About ƱZ
ƱZ is the mysterious masked DJ/producer who originally surfaced in 2012 with his 'Trap Shit' series on Soundcloud which, according to Complex, “took the world by storm with a series of infectious instrumentals” and went on to influence a new era of production. ƱZ has collaborated with artists like Trinidad James, Casey Veggies, Problem, Trae Tha Truth, Iamsu! and Djemba Djemba, and released on tastemaking labels including Fool’s Gold Records, Mad Decent, OWSLA, and Mau5trap. With praise from outlets like SPIN, Billboard, Complex, Hypetrak, and Dancing Astronaut, ƱZ also appears on high profile stages around the world including Coachella, Ultra, Electric Daisy Carnival, Sonar, Stereosonic and more.
