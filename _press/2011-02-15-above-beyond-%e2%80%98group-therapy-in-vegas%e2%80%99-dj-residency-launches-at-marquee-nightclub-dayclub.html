FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

ABOVE & BEYOND

‘GROUP THERAPY IN VEGAS’
DJ RESIDENCY LAUNCHES AT
MARQUEE NIGHTCLUB & DAYCLUB

AT THE COSMOPOLITAN OF LAS VEGAS
ON FEBRUARY 19, 2011


Above & Beyond, the UK’s biggest DJs, will meet the newest revolutionary dance music destination, Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub, for their exclusive U.S. residency ‘Above & Beyond: Group Therapy in Vegas’, launching on Saturday, February 19th, 2011.

Matched with Marquee’s dazzling custom LED visuals, coterie of beautiful costumed dancers, and industry-leading custom sound system, Above & Beyond’s innovative brand of euphoric electronic dance music is sure to lift spirits and feet off the dancefloor. “We've been touring the world and have been amazed how listening to and dancing to music with others helps us through good and tough times in our lives,” says Paavo Siljamäki on the concept behind Group Therapy. From the 8-foot-diameter chandelier to the three-story Dance Tower, Marquee provides the perfect larger-than-life backdrop for Above & Beyond’s exclusive showcase of new material and unreleased anthems from their legendary Anjunabeats imprint. Indeed, Above & Beyond’s exclusive Las Vegas residency promises to be everything their many fans would hope for.

The DJ/production trio, Above & Beyond find every possible platform to bring their emotional trance sound to enthusiastic audiences the world over, with sold out shows, instant classics, and their beloved Trance Around the World radio show with over 30 million listeners each week. The group has racked up countless accolades including Essential Mix of the Year, a New Year’s performance to over 1 million people, as well as prolific production and remix work for artists both mainstream (most famously Madonna’s “What It Feels Like For A Girl”) and underground. Their Anjunabeats andAnjunadeep labels are widely recognized as a premier platform for forward thinking trance and house. Their current International Dance Music Award nominations include ‘Best European DJ’, ‘Best Radio Mix Show DJ’, ‘Best Artist’ (Group), ‘Best DJ Mix’, and ‘Best Podcast’. They recently released10 Years of Anjunabeats, an energizing and uplifting look back over the last decade of the label’s forward-thinking legacy, featuring classic Above & Beyond tracks as well as contributions from Super8 & Tab, Mat Zo, Arty and more. With anticipation building for their second artist album Group Therapy and their lead single “Sun & Moon” recently unveiled, Above & Beyond are poised to provide a truly transcendent experience at Marquee Las Vegas.

Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub at The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas is located at 3708 Las Vegas Blvd. South Las Vegas, NV 89109 and is open four nights a week Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Monday starting at 10 p.m. For more information or reservations please call 702.333.9000.
