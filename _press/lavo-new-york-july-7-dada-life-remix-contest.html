Thursday, July 7 - Dada Life
With their ubiquitous remix of Lady Gaga’s “Born This Way” playing in the hottest clubs across the world, the Swedish duo Dada Life takes their electro house sound to LAVO this month. Dada Life got their first big break in 2006 with “Big Time,” followed by a steady stream of hit singles like “The Great Fashionista Swindle,” “Vote Yes,” and “Fun Fun Fun,” as well as the full length album ‘Just Do the Dada.’  Dada Life’s tunes are played by everyone from Crookers, Eric Prydz and Benny Benassi to David Guetta; Tiësto quickly understood how to Do the Dada – and the duo played before him on one of his massive U.S. tours. Dada Life have also remixed artists such as Laidback Luke, Alex Gopher, Dan Black/Kid Cudi and more -- always creating new remixes and edits to keep in their arsenal for destroying dancefloors and keeping crowds on their feet all night long.

Enter the Dada Life Remix Contest for "White Noise/Red Meat", "Unleash The Fucking Dada", and "Fight Club Is Closed (It’s Time For Rock ‘n’ Roll)". Don't forget the cowbell! Download remix pack here.

Friday, July 8 - Drek Martinez
Born and raised in the South of France, Drek Martinez has established himself as a musical tastemaker and all around nightlife maven in the world’s most vibrant entertainment metropolises. Paris, London, St-Tropez, Milan, and more recently New York, Los Angeles, Toronto, Sao Paulo are among the many cities he has captivated sleek crowds of music aficionados and party revelers with his inimitable gusto, musical ingenuity, and disarming charm. Drek grew up with and evolved to the sounds of DJ AM and Mark Ronson. Today, he plays with some of the music industry’s premier creative masterminds such as A-Trak, Timbaland, Kanye West, Snoop Dogg, Kid Cudi, etc. Drek is also a consummate producer and is currently involved in a number of intriguing personal projects . He has remixed tracks for The Game, Chester French, Nikki and Rich, M.Y.A and many more. Drek is a blazing beacon at the globe’s trendsetting and preferred hotspots of music and nighttime debauchery, always inspiring the crowd and making the soundtrack to everyone’s night unforgettably unique.
