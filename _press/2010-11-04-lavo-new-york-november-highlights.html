FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

LAVO New York
November 2010 Highlights

After an unseasonably warm October, LAVO New York’s state-of-the-art nightclub heated up with world class DJs Chuckie, Tiësto and Erick Morillo playing exclusive nights to packed rooms. This November, LAVO New York continues to bring in some of the best DJ talent to its intimate dancefloor and swank surroundings.

Thursday, November 4th
CALVIN HARRIS
Scottish DJ, producer and in-demand remixer Calvin Harris plays a special mid-week gig amidst his extensive North American tour. The self-styled "King of Electropop" has created hit records for Kylie Minogue, collaborated with Dizzee Rascal, and remixed the likes of Cut Copy, The Ting Tings, Groove Armada, Katy Perry and Kelis. His debut album ‘I Created Disco’ (2007) reached gold status and established him as one of the prime movers on the UK dance scene. His latest album ‘Ready For The Weekend’ (2009) introduced his stadium-sized sound with massive riffs, big hands-in-the-air moments, and almost overnight the album reached #1 in the UK. Now this Brit Award nominee for Best Solo Male brings his massive electropop sound to LAVO’s intimate dancefloor.

Thursday, November 11th
TATIANA FONTES
Brazilian born Tatiana Fontes boasts a diverse selection of careers: international supermodel, Hollywood actress, and now back to her true passion as a DJ. With a father who was a nightclub owner and brothers who were professional DJs, Tatiana grew up in the DJ booth and her affinity for music was a homegrown affair. Her style is a fusion of the 80s and rock from her youth, along with European house and electronic music.

Thursday, November 18th
BEHROUZ
San Francisco native and Miami transplant Behrouz has brought his world famous Pure Behrouz party to audiences on all continents since the early parts of the ‘00s with music spanning the entire spectrum of dance music, from deep house to techno. To date 2010 has been one of Behrouz’ busiest years with a summer residency at Ibiza’s Ushuaia Beach Club and endless gigs around the world. Now Pure Behrouz broadcasts weekly as a 2 hour Sirius XM radio show. Along with an abundance of new studio work and his newest compilation Pure Behrouz: San Francisco on Nervous Records out this Fall, Behrouz cannot be stopped.
