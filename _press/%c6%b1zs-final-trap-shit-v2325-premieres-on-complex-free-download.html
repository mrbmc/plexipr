“Going by the moniker ƱZ, this masked marauder was so onto this "trap shit" that he took the world by storm with a series of infectious instrumentals, thus creating his highly-influential "Trap Sh*t" series.” — Complex.com

 

The mysterious ƱZ releases the final installment of his legendary Trap Shit series with 'Trap Shit V23/25,' premiering on Complex.com, and available for streaming here and free download here. Surfacing in 2012 on Soundcloud, the Trap Shit series caught the attention of tastemaking record labels like Boysnoize Records, Mad Decent, Mau5trap, OWSLA and SMOG, with ƱZ’s tracks going on to feature the likes of Trinidad James, Casey Veggies, Problem & more.

Now with his Trap Shit series coming to an epic close, ƱZ charts fresh territory with a brand new EP featuring four never-before-heard tracks that explore new sounds and influences. Stay tuned for more announcements coming soon.
