dubSpot : redefining music education
New Classes for Spring 2009 Enrollment

DUBSPOT– a world-class electronic music production and DJ institute located in NYC’s Meatpacking District – is now offering open enrollment for Spring 2009. As one of the first Ableton Certified Training Centers in the world, DubSpot has become the premier music school for both aspiring and experienced producers and DJ's.  Check out the new Spring 2009 courses, instructors, and updated curriculum.  Learn Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Reason, Serato, and Native Instruments and class offerings range from beginner to advanced, analog to digital, hardware to software, everything you need to start making music today.  DubSpot is redefining music education for tomorrow’s digital age in their start-of-the-art-facilities.

DUBSPOT & PROPELLERHEAD PRESENT: REASON 4 MASTER CLASS
TUESDAY, APRIL 7th @ 7.30PM | RSVP at info@dubspot.com

DJ & Producer Chris Petti, Propellerhead's US Reason Product Specialist and accomplished musician/producer/dj, will be giving us a behind-the-scene look at music projects he is currently creating in Reason, and will be sharing plenty of useful tips and pointers to supercharge your Reason rack!

In the class, Chris will share knowledge of unlocking the power of Reason's new enhanced sequencer, using shortcuts and tricks to increase workflow, working with Rewire, and looking at power usages of the Thor synthesizer to create stunning original sounds. Whether you are relatively new to working with Reason or a seasoned user, producers of all skill levels will benefit from this informative workshop!

MORE FREE MUSIC WORKSHOPS

Learn the fundamentals of drumming, playing chords, and building song ideas. Explore composing in various popular musical styles and take a look at the methods used to write and arrange complete songs. Designed to increase the overall level of musicianship of our students, these first four workshops will focus on the basics of music, rhythm, and songwriting and will benefit any aspiring DJ and producer.  Space is limited - please RSVP at  djkiva@dubspot.com.
