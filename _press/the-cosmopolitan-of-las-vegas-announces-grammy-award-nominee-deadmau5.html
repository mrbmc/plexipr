The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas announces
GRAMMY Award nominee

deadmau5

BRINGS NEW LIVE PRODUCTION TO The Chelsea
Labor Day Weekend
First Time Full Live Show in Las Vegas
VIP Bottle Service Stage by Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub

Photo Credit: Denise Truscello  

The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas brings world-renowned artist deadmau5 to The Chelsea for two unique shows over Labor Day weekend, Friday, September 2 and Saturday, September 3. With a busy summer headlining some of the biggest festivals in the country, deadmau5 introduces a brand new full live show to the Las Vegas Strip for the first time ever.

Joel Zimmerman, better known as deadmau5, is a dance music phenomenon. The artist’s sets are closer to live performances than DJ sessions as he assembles tracks on the fly using cutting edge computer technology including software that he has helped to write himself. “There are no CDs involved,” the Toronto native explains. “It’s a technological orgy up there and I try and keep it more my music than anyone else’s. If people come out to see deadmau5 I want them to hear deadmau5 music.” This trendsetting artist brings his exciting live show to the world’s newest dance music capital, Las Vegas, and right to The Chelsea, The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas’ state-of-the-art entertainment venue, is located on the fourth floor of the resort.

General Admission tickets are $75, inclusive of taxes and service charges. Tickets are available by calling 702.698.7778 or toll-free 877.551.7778 or online at www.cosmopolitanlasvegas.com. Guests must be 21+ to attend.

VIP Bottle Service Stage is brought to you by Las Vegas nightlife fixture Marquee Nightclub & Dayclub. For table reservations and pricing information, please contact Marquee at 702-333-9000 or e-mail info@marqueelasvegas.com.
