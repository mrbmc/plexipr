FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Sunday, August 2nd, 2009
Dance.Here.Now. in association with Pacha NYC presents a special event
VICTOR CALDERONE

Dance.Here.Now. in association with Pacha NYC presents a special event - Victor Calderone at Water Taxi Beach Governors Island on Sunday, August 2, 2009

Enjoy dancing in the sun, fun, food and spectacular views of downtown Manhattan and the Brooklyn Bridge with musical guest DJ Victor Calderone at the newest Water Taxi Beach along the New York City waterfront. Calderone's DJing expertise and imagination transcend conventional expectations allowing him to effortlessly bridge the gap between multiple genres of music and generations of listeners. From house, tribal and techno, Victor knows how to grab a dancefloor and takes you on a musical journey with just the right amount of sexiness and loads of energy. From his unparalleled Evolve residencies in New York City, Montreal and Los Angeles, chart-topping productions, star-studded remixes, and collaborations with many of music’s most in-demand artists, Victor is a veritable virtuoso who has inspired, enthralled and entertained legions of dance music devotees around the world.

Getting to Governors Island is just a quick, 5-minute boat ride away. The event will be accessible via New York Water Taxi. The Water Taxi's will leave every hour from the Battery Maritime Building Slip 7 on the Southern Tip of Manhattan, located next to the Staten Island Ferry Terminal. The first ferry will leave at 4pm and every 15 minutes thereafter. Additional pickup locations may be added. More information and directions can be found at Water Taxi Beach and Dance.Here.Now.
