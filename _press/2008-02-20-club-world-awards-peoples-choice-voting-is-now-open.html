The Club World Awards people's choice voting is now officially open, via partystrands.com! Clubbers are invited to vote for their favorite local venue in the Nightstalker Award category – a staple of the CWA program since its inception – as well as in music categories Club Banger Of The Year (for instrumentals), Club Anthem Of The Year (for vocals), and Best DJ Set. Online dance music portal Beatport.com will host a Club World Awards page (beatportal.com/clubworldawards) with players, so voters can listen to all the nominated music, including a top 10 of tracks played by the DJs during their nominated sets (provided by them). The inaugural Best DJ Set nominees are BT, Justice, DJ Micro, Roger Sanchez, and Danny Tenaglia. Voting will be hosted on Partystrands.com, and will be live from February 18 through March 3.

Content for the music categories - Club Banger of the Year, Club Anthem of the Uear, + Best DJ set - is on Beatport.com - at www.beatportal.com/clubworldawards. That links to partner site partyStrands - at www.partystrands.com/club-world-awards - for the actual voting. With the 'Results' tab on the partyStrands voting page, you can see in real time who's in the lead.

Info on all our Club World Award nominees is now online at www.clubworldawards.com. Voting closes on March 3!
