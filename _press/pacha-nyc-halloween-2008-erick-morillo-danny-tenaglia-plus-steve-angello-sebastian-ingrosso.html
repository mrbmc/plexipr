Pacha NYC presents an extra special and spooky Halloween marathon event on Friday, October 31st with Pacha co-owner and resident DJ ERIC MORILLO, along with STEVE ANGELLO and SEBASTIAN INGROSSO [Swedish House Mafia], to be followed by none other than NYC's favorite DANNY TENAGLIA, in his triumphant return from Ibiza. Trick or Treat! Show off your costumes at the  with BIGGEST HALLOWEEN COSTUME CONTEST with over $5,000 in cash & prizes, including ERIC MORILLO'S SEXIEST COSTUME CONTEST.

On All Hallows' Eve, two of NYC's biggest DJs Morillo & Tenaglia unite on one of the most extravagant nights of the year for the first time ever at Pacha NYC. Tying it altogether, the Swedish house gods cement the night with their own brand of explosive electro house music with soul. On Saturday morning, legendary NYC DJ Danny Tenaglia will present his annual BOO YOURSELF Halloween party all day long for those seeking out more treats than tricks. This is one Halloween treat you don't want to miss!
