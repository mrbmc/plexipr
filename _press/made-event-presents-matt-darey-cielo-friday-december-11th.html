Friday, December 11, 2009
Made Event presents
MATT DAREY
(Nocturnal Sessions)
with Zack Roth
@ Cielo

Made Event presents the man behind the massively successful Nocturnal Session radio show Matt Darey at Cielo on Friday, December 11, 2009.

Matt Darey is one of the early pioneers of EDM scene spawning countless chart hits and dance anthems over the years. He produces one of the worlds biggest EDM radio shows called Nocturnal Sessions and constantly tours the globe throwing his notoriously crazy Nocturnal parties in major cities every week. Throughout his 17 year career of producing & writing house, trance, breaks and progressive and much more he has sold well over 2 million albums & singles with numerous top 10 & top 20 chart hits.

His Nocturnal radio show is the worlds fastest expanding multi genre dance radio show now broadcast on 116 FM stations worldwide. In recent years Matt has been playing his unique “Nocturnal” blend of many styles including electro, progressive, tech, trance and occasional house & breaks.

He is now back with his first release in over 2 years, bringing together the sound of his guitar band, Urban Astronauts to create the anthem "See The Sun” out now on his brand new label Nocturnal Global with cutting edge trance and progressive house remixes by Aurosonic, Schodt, and Moonbeam, exclusively out on Beatport.

Matt Darey pres. Urban Astronauts - "See The Sun" (remixes) out now on Beatport.
